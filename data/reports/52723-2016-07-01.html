1 He Whakamārama

He Kupu Whakataki

Ka whakahaeretia tētahi arotake tāpiri i runga o te whakataunga ā te Kaiurungi Whakaturuki Arotake Māori i Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga.

E aromātai ana te arotake tāpiri i te whānuitanga me te whai hua o ngā mahi ā te kōhanga reo ki te whakatutuki i ngā take i whakatakotohia ai i roto i te arotake mātauranga o mua atu i tēnei, me ētahi atu wāhanga rānei kua tāutuhia whai muri o taua arotake.

Ngā Whakataunga mō ngā Whakapuakitanga

Ko tēnei arotake tāpiri i whakaritea i runga o te aromātai o te whakatutukitanga a te whānau i te whakahaeretanga o te kōhanga reo, ki ngā wāhanga i tāutuhia ai e te pūrongo a Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga, i mua atu, me ngā take rānei kua tāutuhia whai muri o taua arotake. Ko ngā whakataunga mō ngā whakapuakitanga e pā ana ki tēnei arotake, ko te titiro ki:

  • te whakamahere me te aromātai, tae atu ki ngā tūranga o te whānau me ā rātou kawenga mahi, te whakamahere rautaki, te whakamahere hōtaka, te aromātai me te aromatawai
  • ngā wāhanga kāhore e tutuki ana e ai ki te ture, tae atu ki ngā akoako hōneatanga ohorere me te arohaehae, te whakawhanaketanga ngaio anō hoki mā ngā kaiwhakahaere me ngā kaimahi, me
  • ētahi atu take i tāutuhia ai whai muri i te arotake o mua.

2 Te Horopaki

E tū ana Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Hunga Ririki ki te marae o Te Wairoa Iti, ki Waverley, i Taranaki. He tauhou te nuinga o te whānau ki te kaupapa o te kōhanga reo, ā, kāhore anō rātou kia whakapakari ake i tō rātou mōhiotanga me tō rātou māramatanga ki Te Whāriki me Te Korowai. I tēnei wā, kei te whakamātua pepi te kaiwhakahaere, ā, e whakahaere ana te kaiako i ngā whakaritenga o ia rā, mā te tautoko anō hoki a ngā mema o te whānau.

Ki tā tēnei arotake tāpiri, he whāiti noa te ahu whakamua i ngā wāhanga i tāutuhia ai ki te pūrongo o mua a Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga hei whakapai ake, i te marama o Haratua, i te tau 2015. 

3 Ngā Whakaaturanga

Te hōtaka akoranga

He whakamārama

I tāutu te pūrongo o mua, kua hangahanga rawa, kua ōkawa rawa hoki te hōtaka ako mō ngā tamariki kei raro i te rua o ngā tau, ā, kāhore tēnei e poipoi ana i ō rātou matea ako, i ō rātou ngākaunuitanga, me tō rātou whakawhanaketanga. Kīhai te aromātai hōtaka i whakatutukihia. Kāhore ngā mōhiohio aromatawai i te āta whakarato i ngā mōhiohio e pā ana ki te ako a ngā tamariki me tō rātou whakawhanaketanga.

Ngā wāhanga e pai ana te whakahaere

He pārekareka ki ngā tamariki te ako ki tētahi taiao o te reo Māori. Ahakoa he tokoiti noa ngā tamariki e kōrero ana i te reo Māori, ka whai rātou i ngā tauira o ō rātou hoa me ngā pakeke. Kōrero Māori ai te kaiako ki ngā tamariki i ngā wā katoa. He tauira ia mō ngā tamariki me te whānau. E wātea ana ki ngā tamariki te whānuitanga o ngā tūmomo rauemi. He muramura, he whakaongaonga hoki te taiao ako. He mā te kōhanga reo, ā, e āta tiakina ana. Kua waia ngā tamariki ki ngā mahinga o ia rā. E tino manaakitia ana ngā tamariki i te kōhanga reo.

Ngā Whakaritenga Matua ka Whai Ake

Me whānui ake ngā rerekētanga ki te hōtaka ako mā ngā tamariki. E kōkirihia ana te nuinga o ngā taumahi e ngā pākeke, ā, he iti noa ngā whai wāhitanga mō ngā tamariki ki te whiriwhiri i ā rātou ake kaupapa. Kāhore anō kia rite ngā mahi ki te aromātai hōtaka me te aromatawai, ā, kāhore hoki ēnei e whakatakoto ana i ngā mōhiohio papai mā ngā kaiako, hei hāpai i te whakapaitanga ake o ngā mahi mā ngā tamariki. Nā tēnei, kua whakaritea ngā taumahi mā ngā tamariki, engari kāhore ēnei e akiaki ana i te tūhura me te whakatewhatewha, ā, kei te whakawhāiti anō hoki i te whakawhanaketanga o tō rātou taha auaha. Kei ngā tamariki tētahi hōtaka ako e arotahi ana ki ō rātou ngākaunuitanga me ō rātou matea.

Te whakahaeretanga a te whānau

He whakamārama

I tāutu te pūrongo o mua a Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga i te maha o ngā wāhanga hei whakawhanake ake, me ērā hoki kāhore e tutuki ana e ai ki te ture. Ko ētahi o aua wāhanga, ko ngā akoako hōneatanga ohorere, ko te arohaehae i te āhua mahi a ngā kaimahi, ko te āta whakapūmau i te tatauranga e tika ana ki waenga i ngā pākeke me ngā tamariki, me te whakawhanaketanga ngaio o ngā kaiwhakahaere me ngā kaimahi. Kāhore i te whānau tētahi mahere rautaki. I mate te kōhanga reo ki te whakawhanake tonu i te tūtohinga, i ngā kaupapa here me ngā tukanga.

Ngā wāhanga e pai ana te whakahaere

Kua ahu whakamua te whānau ki te whakatutuki i ngā wāhanga i te pūrongo o mua hei whakawhanake ake. I nāianei, ka āta whakatau te tatauranga ki waenga i ngā pākeke me ngā tamariki i te tika o te mātakitaki tamariki. He tokomaha ngā mema o te whānau e noho mārama ana ki te āhua o ō rātou tūranga me ā rātou kawenga mahi hei mātua ki te kōhanga reo. Kātahi anō tētahi heamana hou ka whakatūngia, ā, he tākare ia ki te ako i tana tūranga hou me āna kawenga. He mahi ohu ā te whānau, ā, ka tū ā rātou hui ia rua wiki hei whakapakari ake i ngā hononga o roto, me ērā hoki ki te marae me te hapori.

Ngā Whakaritenga Matua ka Whai Ake

Me whiwhi te kaiako me ngā mema o te whānau i te whakawhanaketanga ngaio, hei hāpai i tō rātou māramatanga ki ō rātou tūranga me ā rātou kawenga mahi. He iti noa ngā mōhiotanga o ngā kaimahi ki te whakamahere me te whakahaere i ngā hōtaka ako mā ngā tamariki. Kāhore ngā mema hou o te whānau i te mārama ki tā rātou whai wāhi atu hei mātua, hei kaiwhakahaere hoki o tō rātou kōhanga reo. Nā tēnei, he iti noa te ahu whakamua i roto i ngā wāhanga i tāutuhia ai i te pūrongo o mua.

Kāhore i te whānau tētahi tūtohinga e hāngai ana ki tēnei wā. Ahakoa kua whakawhanakehia tētahi mahere rautaki, kāhore he whāinga nahanaha, mārama anō hoki o roto hei aroturuki, hei pūrongo. Me tahuri wawe ki te whakatutuki me te whakawhanake ake tonu i ngā kaupapa here me ngā tukanga. Kāhore e whakatutukihia ana ngā akoako hōneatanga inā he ahi, he rū whenua rānei. Kāhore anō te whānau kia whakatutuki i te arohaehae a ngā kaimahi.

4 Ngā wāhanga kāhore e tutuki ana e ai ki ngā ture

He whakamārama

Hei whakatutuki te whānau o te kōhanga reo i āna herenga ā-ture, me tahuri rātou ki te:

4.1 whakatutuki i ngā akoako hōneatanga ohorere e tika ana ia te wā, ki te taha o ngā tamariki[R46 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; HS8 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]

4.2 whakatinana i tētahi pūnaha arohaehae, whakaratonga hoki mō te whakawhanaketanga ngaio me te ako[R47 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; GMA7 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]

4.3 whakarite i tētahi tauāki tikanga whakaaro e ārahi ana i te kōhanga reo, ā, kia whakarite i tētahi tukanga arotake whaiaro e haere tonu ana hei hāpai i te kōhanga reo ki te whakapūmau me te whakapai ake i te kounga o te mātauranga me te atawhai[R46 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; GMA5, GMA6 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]

4.4 whakarato i tētahi marautanga o te kōhanga reo ko ngā pou o roto ko te whakamahere, ko te whakatinanatanga, me te aromātai hei whakaatu tonu i te mārama ki ngā āhuatanga ako o te mokopuna, ki ō rātou ngākaunuitanga, me ngā horopaki o te whānau me te ao.[R43 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; Paearu c2 o te Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]

5 Ngā Taunakitanga

I whakawhanakehia e Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga me te whānau o te kōhanga reo ngā taunakitanga e whai ake nei, arā, kia tahuri te whānau ki te:

  • toro atu ki te tautoko mātanga, te ārahitanga mātanga hoki mō ngā wāhanga katoa i tāutuhia ai hei whakawhanake ake
  • whakatutuki i ngā wāhanga katoa e tutuki ana e ai ki te ture, me te tuhi haere i ngā whakaaturanga e tohu ana i ngā mahi i whakatutukihia ai.

Te Whakarāpopototanga

E tū ana Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Hunga Ririki ki Waverley, i Taranaki. Ki tā tēnei arotake tāpiri, he iti noa te ahu whakamua ki te whakatutuki i ngā take i whakatakotohia ai ki te pūrongo o mua a Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga, i te Haratua o 2015.

1 Background

Introduction

A supplementary review is undertaken at the discretion of the Deputy Chief Review Officer Māori in the Education Review Office (ERO).

A supplementary review evaluates the extent and effectiveness of actions a kōhanga reo has taken towards addressing issues specified in its previous ERO review and/or any additional areas identified since that review.

Terms of Reference

This supplementary review is based on an evaluation of the performance of the kōhanga reo governing body and management in relation to areas identified in the previous ERO report or issues identified since that review. The terms of reference for this review are to investigate:

  • planning and evaluation including whānau roles and responsibilities, strategic planning, programme planning, evaluation and assessment
  • areas of non-compliance including evacuation and emergency drills and appraisal and professional development for management and kaimahi
  • and any issues identified since the previous review.

2 Context

Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Hunga Ririki is based on Te Wairoa Iti Marae in Waverley, Taranaki. Most of the whānau are new to the kaupapa of kōhanga reo and are yet to build their knowledge and understanding of Te Whāriki and Te Korowai. The kaiwhakahaere is on maternity leave and so the kaiako is managing day to day operations with support from whānau members.

This supplementary review finds limited progress in the areas for improvement identified in the previous ERO education report of May 2015.

3 Findings

Programme for learning

Background

The previous report identified that the learning programme for children under 2 years old was too structured and formal and did not cater for their learning needs, interests and development. Programme evaluation did not happen. Assessment information did not provide enough information about children’s learning and development.

Areas of progress

Children enjoy learning in a te reo Māori environment. Although very few children speak te reo Māori, they follow the examples of their peers and adults. The kaiako speaks te reo Māori with children at all times. She is a role model for both tamariki and whānau. Children have access to a wide range of resources. The learning environment is colourful and stimulating. The kōhanga reo is clean and well maintained. Children are familiar with daily routines. Children are well cared for at kōhanga reo.

Key Next Steps

The learning programme needs more variety for children. Most activities are directed by adults and children have little opportunity to select activities. Programme evaluation and assessment practices are not yet regular nor do they provide good information for the kaiako to help improve what happens for children. As a result, activities organised for children do not encourage exploration and investigation and limits their creative development. Children have a learning programme focused on their interests and needs.

Whānau management

Background

The previous ERO report identified a number of areas for development and non-compliance. These included evacuation and emergency drills, kaimahi performance appraisals, ensuring that adult child ratios are maintained and professional development for management and kaimahi. The whānau did not have a strategic plan. The kōhanga reo charter, policies and procedures required further development.

Areas of progress

The whānau has made some progress to address the areas for development from the previous report. Adult to child rations now ensure that children receive appropriate levels of supervision. Several whānau members have a basic understanding of their roles and responsibilities as kōhanga reo parents. A new chairman has recently been appointed and is eager to familiarise himself with the new role and responsibilities. Whānau have had working bees and fortnightly meetings to build internal relationships and with the local marae and community.

Key next steps

The kaiako and whānau members need professional development to help them understand their roles and responsibilities. Kaimahi have limited knowledge about how to plan and manage learning programmes for children. New whānau members do not understand their position as parents and managers of their kōhanga reo. As a consequence there has been limited progress to areas identified in the previous report.

Whānau do not have a current charter in place. Although a strategic plan has been developed this does not have clear measureable objectives that can then be monitored and reported on. Policies and proceedures require immediate attention and further development. Emergency drills for fire and earthquake are not conducted. Whānau have yet to undertake appraisals for kaimahi.

4 Actions of non-compliance

Overview

In order for the kōhanga reo whānau to meet compliance requirements they need to:

4.1 Carry out relevant evacuation and emergency drills regularly with children[R46 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; HS8 Licensing criteria for kōhanga reo affiliated with Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]

4.2 Implement a system of regular appraisal and provision for professional development and learning[R47 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; GMA7 Licensing criteria for kōhanga reo affiliated with Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]

4.3 Ensure that there is a philosophy statement that guides the kōhanga and that there is an ongoing process of self-review to help the kōhanga maintain and improve the quality of its education and care[R46 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; GMA5, GMA6 Licensing criteria for kōhanga reo affiliated with Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]

4.4 Provide a Kōhanga reo curriculum informed by planning, implementation, and evaluation that demonstrates an understanding of mokopuna learning, their interests, whānau and life contexts.[R43 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; Criterion c2 Licensing criteria for kōhanga reo affiliated with Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]

5 Recommendations

ERO and the kōhanga reo whānau developed the following recommendations for the whānau to:

  • seek specialist support and guidance for all identified areas for development
  • address areas of non-compliance and document evidence of the actions taken.

Conclusion

Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Hunga Ririki is in Waverley, Taranaki. This supplementary review finds limited progress has been made towards addressing the issues raised in the previous ERO report of May 2015.

6 When is ERO likely to review the kōhanga reo again?

The next ERO review of Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Hunga Ririki will be within one year.

Lynda Pura-Watson

Deputy Chief Review Officer Māori
