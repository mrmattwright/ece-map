Te Aromātai

E tū ana Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Whare o Wikitoria ki te marae o Waipatu, ki Heretaunga. Kāhore i te rawaka te whakatau i ngā whakaritenga, he ngoikore ngā hononga ki waenga i ngā pākeke, kāhore i te tika te poipoi i ngā tamariki, ā, kei te pā tōraro ēnei āhuatanga ki te kōhanga reo. I ētahi wā, kāhore ētahi o ngā tamariki e tino tautokona ana i roto i ā rātou akoranga.

Ka arotake anō Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga i Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Whare o Wikitoria i roto i te kotahi tau.

1 Te Horopaki

E tū ana Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Whare o Wikitoria ki te marae o Waipatu, ki Heretaunga.

I te wā o te arotake, he tino take āwangawanga i whakapuakihia e ētahi o ngā whānau me ngā kaimahi. I hāngai aua take ki te atawhai me te waiora o ngā tamariki, te whakahaeretanga o ngā kaimahi, te marautanga, me te whakahaeretanga o te kōhanga reo.

He pēhea rawa te kite ki ngā tamariki kei te kaha tautokona, kei te kaha manaakitia hoki rātou i roto i ā rātou akoranga?

Kāhore i te rawaka te whakatau i ngā whakaritenga, he ngoikore ngā hononga ki waenga i ngā pākeke, kāhore i te tika te poipoi i ngā tamariki, ā, kei te pā tōraro ēnei āhuatanga ki te kōhanga reo. I ētahi wā, kāhore ētahi o ngā tamariki e tino tautokona ana i roto i ā rātou akoranga.

2 Ngā Whakaaturanga o te Arotake

Ngā Taumata Whakahirahira

He pai te whakawhanaketanga o ngā tamariki i a rātou e ako ana i te reo Māori. Ka whai wāhi atu rātou ki ngā whakawhitinga kōrero māmā ki waenga i a rātou anō me ngā pākeke. Ka whai wāhi ngā tamariki ki ngā taumahi reo ōkawa pērā i ngā karakia, ngā hīmene, ngā waiata, me ngā mihimihi. Ka whakarongo rātou ki te reo, ā, ka whai wāhi atu hoki ki ngā tikanga Māori i runga i te marae. Ka hāpai ngā kaimahi i te whakawhanake tonutanga o te reo Māori o ngā tamariki. He māia te whakamahinga a ngā tamariki i te reo Māori.

Ka ako ngā tamariki ki tētahi wāhi i hangaia ai hei taiao akoranga, ā, kua āta waihangatia. Kei te kōhanga reo te maha o ngā wāhi hei whakamahi. He pouaka māra, he taonga tākaro hoki ki waho, ā, ka kōkiri ngā tamariki i te whakamahinga, ka āwhina rānei ngā kaimahi. Ko te māra a te hapori tētahi āhuatanga motuhake ki tēnei marae. Ka whai wāhi te whānau me ngā tamariki ki te tiaki i te māra me te hauhake i ngā kai. He pakari ngā tamariki ki te whakamahi i ngā rauemi me ngā wāhi tākaro.

Te Whakamahere me te Aromātai

He pārekareka ki ngā tamariki ngā wheako hihiri e whai pūtake ana, ā, ka whakatairanga ēnei i ā rātou akoranga. He tirohanga tā ngā kaimahi mō ia wāhanga, ā, ka whakamahia e rātou hei whakamahere i ngā tino wheako akoranga. Ka āta toro atu hoki rātou ki ngā taonga o te taiao, hei whakaako ki ngā tamariki ngā whakamahinga tuku iho, ngā whakamahinga hoki o te ao hou o te whenua. He pārekareka ki ngā tamariki ngā wheako reo matatini me ngā wheako pāngarau e hāpai ana i tā rātou whakarite ki te whakawhiti atu ki te kura. Ka whakawhiti kōrero ngā kaimahi mō te hōtaka me te aro nui o ngā rōpū tamariki me ia tamaiti. He pārekareka ki ngā tamariki te ako.

Ka whai whakaaro ki ngā matea hinengaro o ngā tamariki. Kei te tino mōhio te nuinga o ngā kaimahi ki ngā tamariki. Ka kōrero rātou mō ngā pūmanawa me ngā ngākau nuitanga o ia tamaiti, ā, ka aroturuki i tā rātou ahu whakamua. Ka whakapuaki ngā kaimahi i tēnā e mōhiotia ana e rātou mō ngā tamariki ki ō rātou mātua.

Ngā Whakaritenga Matua ka Whai Ake

Kāhore ngā tamariki kei te pūtake o ngā whakataunga a te whānau. Kāhore i te mārama ki te whānau te pūtake o te rōpū kāwanatanga me tā rātou kawenga ki te whakahaere i te kōhanga reo. Kāhore i te mārama ngā tukanga e pā ana ki te whakatau whakaritenga. Kāhore ngā kaupapa here me ngā tukanga e āta whakamahia ana hei ārahi i ngā tukanga ka hāpai ake i te whakahaeretanga me te whakaritenga o te kōhanga reo. Kei te pā tōraro tēnei ki ngā tamariki.

Kei te noho āwangawanga te waiora ā-whatumanawa o ngā tamariki. Kua whakatairangatia ngā tino āwangawanga mō te pā tōraro tonu o ētahi o ngā hononga, me te rongo o ngā tamariki i te taumaha o aua taunekeneke. He ngoikore ngā tukanga e pā ana ki te whakahaeretanga o ngā amuamu me te āhua o ngā mahi a ngā kaimahi. Kei te tārewa tonu ngā amuamu, ā, kei te whakawhēuaua ētahi o te whānau ki te whakapuaki i ō rātou āwangawanga. Ka rongo ngā tamariki i ētahi wā, i te whanonga hē o ngā pākeke.

I ētahi wā kāhore te waiora ā-tinana o ngā tamariki e āta poipoi tōtikatia ana. Kāhore he kaupapa here mārama, he tukanga mārama rānei hei ārahi i te whakamahinga o ngā rautaki papai mō te whakahaere i ngā tamariki. Ka whakamahi ētahi o ngā kaimahi i ngā rautaki whakahaere whanonga kāhore i te tika, ā, ka manawapā ngā tamariki. He wā anō kua noho taratahi ngā tamariki, ā, kāhore i te mātakina. Ka mānenei te taunekeneke a ngā tamariki ki te taha o ētahi o ngā pākeke.

3 Te Whakataunga a te Kōhanga Reo ki ngā Wāhanga Tautukunga

I te wā o te arotake, i whakamātauhia e Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga ngā pūnaha o te kōhanga reo ki te whakahaere i ngā āhuatanga i raro iho nei e whai pānga nui ana ki te waiora o ngā tamariki:

  • te haumaru whatumanawa (tāpiri atu ko te ārahi mauritau, ko te ārai tamariki)
  • te haumaru ā-tinana (tāpiri atu ko te mātakitaki tamariki; ko ngā whakaritenga whakamoe; ko ngā aituā; ko te whāngai rongoā; ko ngā whakaritenga akuaku; ko ngā kaupapa here me ngā tikanga haerenga whakawaho)
  • te tū tika o ngā kaimahi (tae atu ki ngā taumata tohu mātauranga; te arowhai a ngā pirihimana; ngā rēhitatanga kaiako; me te tatauranga ki waenga i te kaiako me te tamaiti)
  • ngā whakaritenga, tikanga hōneatanga e pā ana ki te ahi me te rū whenua.

Ko te tikanga, ka whakatairanga ngā kōhanga reo katoa i te hauora me te haumaru o ngā tamariki, me te arotake anō hoki i ia te wā i tā rātou whakatutukitanga i ngā herenga ā-ture.

I te wā o te arotake a Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga, i tāutuhia ai ngā wāhanga e whai ake nei, kāhore e whakatutuki ana i ngā herenga ā-ture. Hei whakapai ake i ngā whakaritenga, me tahuri te whānau ki te:

  • whakarite i te pakari o ngā tukanga me ngā mahi tuhituhi hei aroturuki i ngā wā moe o ngā tamariki[R 46 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008 HS 1-3, 9]
  • whakawhanake i tētahi tukanga ka whakatau i ngā amuamu e pā ana ki ngā herenga ā-ture kāhore e tutukihia ana, nā runga i te āhua o ngā herenga, ngā paearu raihana rānei[R 47) Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008 GMA 1]
  • whakarite kia kore rawa ai ngā tamariki e rongo i ngā tūmomo whanonga ka whakararu pea i a rātou[R 46 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008 HS31-33, C10]
  • whakarato i te mātauranga me te atawhai ka whakatairanga i ngā akoranga a ngā mokopuna mā ngā taunekeneke papai e whai pūtake ana.[R 41 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; C1-4 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]

4 Ngā Taunakitanga

E taunaki ana Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga kia mahi ngātahi te whānau me ngā kaimahi ki Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo, ki te whakatutuki i ngā take i tāutuhia ai ki tēnei pūrongo.

Te Whakarāpopototanga

E tū ana Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Whare o Wikitoria ki te marae o Waipatu, ki Heretaunga. I ētahi wā, kāhore ētahi o ngā tamariki e tino tautokona ana i roto i ā rātou akoranga.

Findings

Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Whare o Wikitoria is at Waipatu Marae in Hastings. Inadequate decision making, poor relationships between adults, inappropriate management of children, impact negatively. There are some instances where some children are not well supported in their learning.

The next ERO review of Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Whare o Wikitoria will be within one year.

1 Context

Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Whare o Wikitoria is at Waipatu Marae, Hastings.

During the course of the review significant concerns were expressed by some whānau and staff. These concerns included children’s care and wellbeing, personnel management, curriculum and kōhanga reo management.

To what extent do children show that they are well supported and cared for in their learning?

Inadequate decision making, poor relationships between adults, inappropriate management of children, impact negatively. There are some instances where some children are not well supported in their learning.

2 The Review Findings

Ngā Taumata Whakahirahira

Children are developing well as they learn te reo Māori. They engage in simple conversations with each other and adults. Children are a part of formal language activities including karakia, hīmene, waiata and mihimihi. They listen to te reo and experience tikanga Māori on the marae. Staff support children’s ongoing development of te reo Māori. Children use te reo Māori with confidence.

Children learn in a well-designed, purpose built learning environment. The kōhanga reo includes many usable spaces. Outside there are planter boxes and play equipment which the children use independently or with staff. The community garden is a unique feature located on the marae. Whānau and children help to care for the garden and harvest crops. Children confidently use resources and play spaces.

Planning and Evaluation

Children enjoy interesting and relevant experiences that enhance their learning. Kaimahi have an overview of each term and they use this to plan specific learning experiences. They make the most of the physical and natural environment to teach children about traditional and contemporary uses of the land. Children enjoy literacy and numeracy experiences that supports their preparation for school. Kaimahi talk about the programme and how groups and individual children are responding. Children enjoy learning.

Children’s intellectual needs are considered. Most kaimahi know the children well. They talk about the strengths and interests of individual children and monitor their progress. Kaimahi share what they know about children with their parents.

Key Next Steps

Children, are not the focus of whānau decision making. Whānau are unclear about the purpose of the governance group and their responsibility in managing the kōhanga reo. Decision making processes are not clear. Policy and procedures are not used effectively to guide processes that support kōhanga management and operation. Children are disadvantaged.

Children’s emotional wellbeing is at risk. Serious concerns have been raised about ongoing negative relationships and the way that children are exposed to tense interactions. Processes for managing complaints and staff performance are poor. Complaints are left unresolved and some whānau are reluctant to raise their concerns. Children are exposed to some inappropriate behaviour of adults.

Children’s physical wellbeing is compromised at times. There is no clear policy or procedure to guide the use of positive strategies for managing children. Some staff use inappropriate behaviour management techniques that cause distress to children. There are instances where children have been isolated and left unattended. Children interact tentatively with some adults.

3 Whānau Management Assurance on Compliance Areas

During the review, ERO looked at the kōhanga reo systems for managing the following areas that have a potentially high impact on children's wellbeing:

  • emotional safety (including positive guidance and child protection)
  • physical safety (including supervision; sleep procedures; accidents; medication; hygiene; excursion policies and procedures)
  • suitable staffing (including qualification levels; police vetting; teacher registration; ratios)
  • evacuation procedures and practices for fire and earthquake.

All kōhanga reo are required to promote children's health and safety and to regularly review their compliance with legal requirements.

During the course of ERO’s review, the following areas of non-compliance were identified. In order to improve practice the whanau must:

  • ensure there are robust processes and recording practices in place for monitoring children’s sleep habits[R46 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; Licensing Criteria for Kohanga Reo affiliated with Te Kohanga Reo National Trust 2008, HS 1-3, 9]
  • develop a procedure to deal with complaints about non-compliance with the regulations or licensing criteria[R47 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; Licensing Criteria for Kohanga Reo affiliated with Te Kohanga Reo National Trust 2008, GMA 1]
  • ensure that children are not subjected to any form of behaviour that presents harm to them[R46 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; Licensing Criteria for Kohanga Reo affiliated with Te Kohanga Reo National Trust 2008, HS31-33, C10]
  • provide education and care to enhance mokopuna learning through meaningful and positive interactions.[R41 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008;C1-4 Licensing Criteria for Kōhanga Reo affiliated with Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]

4 Recommendation

ERO recommends that whānau and staff work with the TKRNT to address the issues identified in this report.

Conclusion

Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Whare o Wikitoria is at Waipatu Marae in Hastings. Inadequate decision making, poor relationships between adults, inappropriate management of children, impact negatively. There are some instances where some children are not well supported in their learning.

When is ERO likely to review the kōhanga reo again?

The next ERO review of Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Whare o Wikitoria will be within one year.
