The Review Findings

Children were observed to be confident, active and engaged in their learning and play. They are able to make choices about their learning and are able to sustain involvement in a wide range of learning experiences.

The learning environment both inside and outside is safe, very well resourced and attractively presented to support children‚Äôs learning. Particular features of the environment include

The kindergarten, with the support of the professional leader, is in the process of reviewing appraisal procedures to ensure that teachers are able to monitor and review their practice over time.

Management Assurance on Legal Requirements

Before the review, the staff and management of Central Kids Kindergartens - Te Kuiti completed an ERO Centre Assurance Statement and Self-Audit Checklist. In these documents they attested that they have taken all reasonable steps to meet their legal obligations related to:

  • curriculum
  • premises and facilities
  • health and safety practices
  • governance, management and administration.

During the review, ERO looked at the service‚Äôs systems for managing the following areas that have a potentially high impact on children's wellbeing:

  • evacuation procedures and practices for fire and earthquake.

All early childhood services are required to promote children's health and safety and to regularly review their compliance with legal requirements.
