Key Next Steps

The service owner agrees that key next steps to improve the quality of management include:

  • establishing a robust process of self review that includes systematic policy review, is evaluative and focused on outcomes for children

  • ensuring a person responsible is available at all times the service is operating for children

  • developing a strategic plan to guide ongoing improvement

Management Assurance on Legal Requirements

Before the review, the staff and management of AKO-hia Home-based ECE Service completed an ERO Home-based Education and Care Assurance Statement and Self-Audit Checklist. In these documents they attested that they have taken all reasonable steps to meet their legal obligations related to:

  • curriculum
  • premises and facilities
  • health and safety practices
  • governance, management and administration.

Actions for compliance

ERO identified areas of non-compliance. To meet requirements the service needs to improve its performance in the following areas:

  • ensure systems for monitoring hazards, health and safety procedures and other legal requirements in homes are monitored and documented.

  • establish documented self review

  • ensure a person responsible is available at all times the service is operating

  • establish a system of regular appraisal

ERO recommends that the service consult with the Ministry of Education and plan to address the key next steps and actions outlined in this report.
