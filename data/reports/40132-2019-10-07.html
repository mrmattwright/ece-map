1 He Whakamārama

He Kupu Whakataki

Ka whakahaeretia tētahi aromātai tāpiri i runga o te whakataunga ā te Toihau Arotake i Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga.

E aromātai ana Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga i te whānuitanga me te whai hua o ngā mahi ā te kōhanga reo ki te whakatutuki i ngā take i whakatakotohia ai i roto i te pūrongo aromātai mātauranga o mua atu i tēnei, me ētahi atu wāhanga rānei i tāutuhia ai whai muri o taua aromātai.

Ngā Whakataunga mō ngā Whakapuakitanga

Ko tēnei aromātai tāpiri i whakaritea i runga i te aromātai o te whakatutukitanga a te whānau i te whakahaeretanga o te kōhanga reo, ki ngā wāhanga i tāutuhia ai e te pūrongo a Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga, i mua atu, me ngā take rānei kua tāutuhia whai muri o taua aromātai. Ko ngā whakataunga mō ngā whakapuakitanga e pā ana ki tēnei aromātai, ko te titiro ki:

  • te whakamahere me te aromātai – te whakawhanake i tētahi marautanga e whakatutuki ana i ngā matea o ngā tamariki
  • ngā wāhanga kāhore e tutuki ana e ai ki te ture, tae atu ki ngā akoako ohorere, te arowhai i ngā āhuatanga mōrearea, te aroturuki i ngā wā moe, ngā mahi arowhai kaimahi a ngā pirihimana, te arohaehae, me
  • ētahi atu take i tāutuhia ai whai muri i te arotake o mua.

2 Te Horopaki

E tū ana Te Kōhanga Reo o Taupō ki te tāone o Taupō. Nō nā tata tonu nei te whānau i tau anō ai ki tō rātou whare, whai muri i te noho tahi ki reira me tētahi atu kōhanga reo. Kātahi anō te kaiako ka whakatūngia, ā, e mahi tahi ana me te whānau ki te whakapakari ake i ngā pūkenga e hāpai ake ai i te ako a ngā tamariki, tae atu hoki ki tō rātou waiora, tō rātou hauora, me tō rātou noho haumaru.

3 Ngā Whakaaturanga

Kua whakatakotohia ngā tukanga e pā ana ki te whakamahere, te aromatawai, me te aromātai. Ka whakamahere ngātahi ngā kaiako ki ngā hui ā-kaimahi e tū ana i ia te wā. E ākina ana te whai wāhi mai o te whānau ki te whakamaheretanga o tētahi hōtaka e whai pānga nui ana ki ā rātou tamariki. He ngohengohe ngā mahere kia whai wāhi mai ai hoki ērā o ngā wheako ako ka pupū noa ake. Ko tētahi tauira o taua āhuatanga nō nā noa mai, ko te take taiao, take ahurea hoki i rewa whawhati tata ai nā te paruparu i tukua atu ki tō rātou moana. E tohu ana te ahu whakamua nei i te whakapaitanga ake.

Ko ngā kaimahi kātahi anō ka whakatūngia e whai hua ana ki te kōhanga reo, ā, ki te taha hoki o ngā tamariki me te whānau. He pai kē atu i nāianei te hāpai i ngā kaimahi ki te whakapakari ake i te āheinga ki te whakatinana i tētahi hōtaka akoranga e tika ana. Kei ngā puka aromatawai ngā mōhiohio e pā ana ki te ahu whakamua a ngā tamariki me ō rātou whakawhanaketanga. Ka āta kitea tēnā e ākona ana e ngā tamariki ki ngā paki ako me ngā pikitia. E whakapuakihia ana aua mōhiohio ki te whānau. Ka āta whakaarohia ngā matea ako o ngā tamariki me ngā kaupapa matua.

Kua pakari ake te māramatanga o ngā kaimahi ki te aromātai i te hōtaka. Ka whakawhiti kōrero rātou mō te āhua o te hōtaka, me ngā āhuatanga hoki hei whakapai ake. Koia nei hoki tētahi atu wāhanga e hāpaitia ai, hei whakapakari ake i ngā āheinga o ngā kaimahi.

Ngā wāhanga kāhore e tutuki ana e ai ki te ture

Ngā wāhanga e ahu whakamua ana

Kua whakaraupapahia te haumaru ā-tinana o ngā tamariki hei kaupapa matua. Kua whakatakotohia ngā tukanga e pā ana ki ngā akoako hōneatanga ohorere, te aroturuki i ngā wā moe, te arowhai i ngā āhuatanga mōrearea, me ngā mahi arowhai kaimahi a ngā pirihimana. Kei te māramahia e ngā kaimahi, he mea nui ki te whakapūmau i aua whakaritenga me ngā tūmomo tuhinga e whai pānga ana.

Kua pai ake te whakahaere i ngā kaimahi. Kua whakatīmatahia tētahi pūnaha arohaehae. I toro atu te whānau ki ngā mātanga o waho hei hāpai i taua tūāhuatanga. He tautōhito te kuia e arohaehae ana i ngā kaimahi. E tāutuhia ana ngā pūmanawa me ngā matea whakawhanaketanga ngaio o ngā kaimahi. E whakamanahia ana, e tautokona ana hoki ngā kaimahi.

Ngā wāhanga hei whakawhanake tonu

Me whakawhanake tonu i te arotake o roto, puta noa i ngā whakahaeretanga. Kāhore anō te whānau kia tāutu i ā rātou whāinga mō te paetawhiti, ā, i tētahi mahere hoki kia whakatutukihia ai aua whāinga. Kāhore anō kia whakaritea te arotake auau o ngā kaupapa here me ngā take whakahaere. I te whānau e waia haere ana ki tō rātou noho takitahi atu ki tō rātou kōhanga reo, e tau ana hoki ki a rātou, e tika ana kia aro matua atu ki aua whakaritenga. Me whakapiki ake i te tatauranga o ngā tamariki ki te rārangi ingoa, ā, me rapu hoki i ngā huarahi e whakapakari ake ai i te rōnakitanga o tō rātou kōhanga reo.

4 Ngā wāhanga kāhore e tutuki ana e ai ki te ture

I tāutuhia e Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga ngā wāhanga kāhore e tutuki ana e ai ki te ture. Kia tutukihia ai e te whānau o te kōhanga reo ā rātou herenga ā-ture, me tahuri rātou ki te:

  • whakarite i tētahi tukanga aromātai o roto ka haere tonu i ia te wā, hei hāpai i te kōhanga reo ki te whakapūmau me te whakapai ake i te kounga o ngā whakaritenga mā ngā tamariki
    [R47 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; GMA6 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]
  • whakarite i tētahi mahere ā-tau hei ārahi i ngā whakahaeretanga o te kōhanga reo.
    [R47 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; GMA8 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]

5 Te Taunakitanga

Kia toro atu ki te tautoko o te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo, ki te whakapakari tonu i te āheinga o te whānau ki te āta whakarite i te whakahaeretanga me te rōnakitanga o te kōhanga reo.

6 Hei ā hea Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga aromātai anō ai i te kōhanga reo?

Ka whakahaere anō Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga i te aromātai whai muri o Te Kōhanga Reo o Taupō i roto i te toru o ngā tau.

1 Background

Introduction

A supplementary evaluation is undertaken at the discretion of the Chief Review Officer of the Education Review Office (ERO).

ERO evaluates the extent and effectiveness of actions a kōhanga reo has taken towards addressing issues specified in its previous ERO evaluation and/or any additional areas identified since that evaluation.

Terms of Reference

This supplementary evaluation is based on an evaluation of the performance of the kōhanga reo governing body and management in relation to areas identified in the previous ERO report or issues identified since that evaluation. The terms of reference for this evaluation area to investigate:

  • planning and evaluation – develop a curriculum that meets the needs of tamariki
  • areas of non-compliance including emergency drills, hazard checks, monitoring sleep, police vetting and appraisal, and
  • any issues identified since the previous review.

2 Context

Te Kōhanga Reo o Taupō is situated in Taupō. The kōhanga whānau has recently re-settled into their building since sharing their facilities with another kōhanga reo. The newly appointed kaiako is working closely with whānau to build capability to improve children’s learning, wellbeing, health and safety.

3 Findings

Planning, assessment and evaluation processes are established. Kaiako plan together at regular kaimahi hui. Whānau are encouraged to contribute towards planning a programme that suits their children. Planning is flexible to allow spontaneous learning experiences to be incorporated. A recent example of this is the unexpected environmental and cultural impact of waste being dumped into their moana. This progress shows improvement.

Newly appointed staff have had a positive impact on the kōhanga, children and whānau. Kaimahi are now better supported to build capability in implementing a learning programme that is appropriate. Assessment booklets contain information on children’s progress and development. Stories and pictures clearly show what children are learning. This information is shared with whanau. Children’s learning needs and priorities are considered.

Kaimahi have improved understanding of programme evaluation. They discuss how the programme is going and how it can be improved. This is another area in which kaimahi are being supported to build capability.

Areas of non-compliance

Areas of progress

Children’s physical safety has been prioritised. Processes have been established for; emergency evacuation drills, sleep monitoring, hazard checking and police vetting. Kaimahi understand the importance of maintaining these practices and the relevant documentation.

Personnel management has improved. An appraisal system has been started. Whānau sought external expertise to help with this. An experienced kuia is appraising kaimahi. Kaimahi strengths and professional development needs are being identified. Kaimahi feel valued and supported.

Areas for further improvement

Internal review across operations requires further development. Whānau have yet to identify their long-term goals and a plan to achieve them. Regular review of policies and operational matters has not been established. As whānau adjust to not sharing facilities they are aware that this planning is a priority. There is a need to increase the number of children on the roll and look at how to ensure sustainability for their Kōhanga reo.

4 Actions of non-compliance

Overview

ERO identified areas of non-compliance. In order for the kōhanga reo whānau to meet compliance requirements they need to:

  • Whānau establish an ongoing process of self- review to help maintain and improve the quality of practice for children
    [Regulation 47 (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; GMA6 Licensing Criteria for Kōhanga Reo affiliated with Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]
  • Whānau establish an annual plan to guide the operations of the kōhanga reo.
    [Regulation 47 (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; GMA8 Licensing Criteria for Kōhanga Reo affiliated with Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]

5 Recommendation

Seek support from Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust to continue to build capability of the whānau to ensure sound management and sustainability of the Kōhanga reo.

6 When is ERO likely to evaluate the kōhanga reo again?

The next ERO evaluation of Te Kōhanga Reo o Taupō will be in three years.
