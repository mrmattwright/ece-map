1 He Whakamārama

He Kupu Whakataki

Ka whakahaeretia tētahi arotake tāpiri i runga o te whakataunga ā tētahi ō ngā Kaiwhakahaere Matua ā-Rohe o te Tari Arotake Mātauranga.

E aromātai ana te arotake tāpiri i te whānuitanga me te whai hua o ngā mahi ā te kōhanga ki te whakatutuki i ngā take i whakatatokohia i roto i te arotake mātauranga o mua atu i tēnei, me ētahi atu wāhanga rānei, kua tāutuhia whai muri o taua arotake.

Ngā Whakataunga mō ngā Whakapuakitanga

Ko tēnei arotake tāpiri i whakaritea i runga o te aromātaitanga o te whakatutukitanga a te rōpū kāwanatanga, whakahaeretanga o He Iti Nā Motai, e pā ana ki ngā wāhanga i tāutuhia e te pūrongo a te Tari Arotake Mātauranga, ite marama o Hereturikōkā te tau 2010, me ngā take rānei kua tāutuhia whai muri o taua arotake. Ko ngā whakataunga mō ngā whakapuakitanga e pā ana ki tēnei arotake, ko te titiro ki:

te kounga o te mātauranga, te whakahaere whanonga, te whakahaere kaimahi, te hautū i te kōhanga, ngā whakamahere ā-tau, ā-rautaki, te kaupapa o te pokapū me āna mahi, te whakatakoto tahua putea ā-tau

ngā wāhanga kāore i te tutuki ki ngā hanganga ture tae atu ki te tuhi i te hoatu rongoā me te whakarite i tētahi wāhi tika hei tīni i ngā kope o ngā pēpi.

2 Te Aromātai a te Tari Arotake Mātauranga

Ko He Iti Nā Motai he pokapū kōhungahunga rumaki ki te reo Māori kei Te Wānanga o Raukawa ki Otaki. I te wa o te arotake a te Tari Arotake Mātauranga i te tau 2010, i te whai te pokapū ki te whakatika i ētahi take whakahaere, take kaimahi hoki e hāngai ana ki te rōpū whakahaere o taua wā, ā, ki ngā mahi ā ngā kaimahi. Kua mātua whakatūria te pokapū i nāianei hei wāhanga tūturu o Te Wānanga o Raukawa. Kua tau te angitū o ngā mahi ā te rōpū whakahaere o te pokapū, ā ngā kaimahi me te whānau ki te whakakaha i ngā wāhanga arotake, whanaketanga i tāutuhia ki te pūrongo a te Tari Arotake o mua atu nei. Nā tēnei kua pai ake ngā mahi ā te pokapū, ā, me te hōtaka mā ngā tamariki.

Kua mahi tahi ngā mema o te rōpū whakahaere, ngā kaimahi me te whānau ki te whakatutuki i tētahi māramatanga kotahi e pā ana ki te kaupapa me ngā mātāpono o te pokapū. He rite tonu ngā whakawhitinga kōrero i waenganui o te whānau e pā ana ki te whakatinana i te kaupapa, ngā mātāpono o te hōtaka me ngā mahi ā ngā kaimahi.

Mai i te arotake a te Tari Arotake Mātauranga o mua atu i tēnei kua whakatūria tētahi kaihautū mō te pokapū. Kua whakaratohia ki ngā kaimahi te whānui o ngā akoranga me ngā whakawhanaketanga ngaio. I arotahi ēnei ki te whakatutuki i tētahi hōtaka whakahaere ōrite, te whakatinana i te kaupapa me te tiaki tamariki. Ka āta aroturukihia ēnei wāhanga e te kaiwhakahaere me te rōpū whakahaere i te pokapū.

Ka whakamaheretia ngā whāinga ā-tau. Ka tūhono hāngai ēnei ki te tirohanga rautaki me te ahunga o te pokapū. Kua tāutuhia ngā whāinga matua i runga o te tātaritanga o ngā pūmanawa me ngā matea o ngā mema o te pokapū. Kei te tuku pūrongo te rōpū whakahaere e pā ana ki te mahi ngātahi ki te aroturuki me te whakahaere i te tahua ā-tau me te whakapaunga o te pūtea.

E tūtohu ana Tari Arotake Mātauranga kia whakapakari tonu ngā mema o te pokapū i ngā mahi arotake ōkawa me te tuku pūrongo i runga o ngā mahi puta noa i ngā whakahaerenga me ngā mahi katoa āq te pokapū.

Te Whakaritenga Whaimuri

E whakaae ana te Tari Arotake Mātauranga kei te whakahaeretia te pokapū hei painga mō ngā tamariki. Nō reira, ka arotakehia anō e te Tari Arotake Mātauranga te pokapū hei wāhanga o te huringa arotake o te wā.

3 Ngā Whakaaturanga

Te Kounga o te Matāuranga

He whakamārama

I tāutuhia e te pūrongo a te Tari Arotake Mātauranga o Hereturikōkā 2010 ētahi wāhanga hei whanake, hei arotake. Tae atu ki te take kia whakawhanake ngā kaimahi me te whānau i te māramatanga ngātahi e pā ana ki tā rātou kaupapa, te whakatū i tētahi kaihautū ngaio hei whakanui ake i te tōtika o ngā mahi ā te pokapū, te whakahaere kaimahi tae atu ki ngā aromihi kaimahi me te hōtaka. E tika ana kia kaha ake te aroturuki i te tahua. Ko te arotake i te kaupapa here mō te ārahi tōtika hei whakapai ake i ngā tukanga whakahaere whanonga tētahi atu āwangawanga hei whakatika. Mai i te arotake o mua a te Tari Arotake Mātauranga i pukumahi ngā mema o te pokapū kia ahu whakamua i runga o ngā wāhanga i tāutuhia hei whakawhanaketanga mā rātou.

Ngā wāhanga kua pai ake

Te tūhonohono i te kaupapa me te tirohanga whānui ki ngā mahi ā te pokapū. Kei te mārama pai i naiānei ki ngā mema o te pokapū me te whānau te kaupapa me te tirohanga whānui a te pokapū. He nui ngā mahi kua oti i ngā kaimahi me te rōpū whakahaere ki te tūhono i tā rātou kaupapa ki ngā mahi ā te pokapū. Kua uru ngā kaimahi ki te maha o ngā akoranga me ngā whanaketanga ngaio hei whakatutuki i tēnei. Kua tāpirihia te whakamahere me te kawe i te hōtaka, me te aromatawai ki ngā whakawhanaketanhga me ngā akoranga ngaio. Ka whai whakaaro ngā kaimahi ki ēnei mahi i ia te wā me te tāpiri i ēnei whakaaro ki te whanaketanga o te hōtaka. Ka whakamōhio ngā mema o te whānau i ia te wā ki te kaupapa me te tirohanga whānui mā ngā pānui ā te whānau me ngā kete kohi kōrero whanaketanga ā ngā tamariki.

Te whakamahere e ai ki ngā whāinga matua. Ka whakamaheretia ngā whāinga matua ā-tau me te tūhono i ēnei ki te mahere rautaki a te pokapū. Kei te tino hāngai te whakamahere whāinga matua ā-tau ki te whanaketanga o ngā kaimahi.

Te tātaki me te ahunga o te pokapū. Mai i te arotake a te Tari Arotake Mātauranga o mua atu nei, kua whakatūria e te rōpū whakahaere tētahi kaiwhakahaere hei ārahi i ngā mahi ā te pokapū, me te whakahaere kaimahi, te whanaketanga o te hōtaka. Kua pā takatika tēnei ki ngā mahi whakahaere kaimahi me te hōtaka akoranga ā ngā tamariki.

Te whakapaunga pūtea. Kei ngā kaiwhakahaere o te pokapū tētahi tahua ā-tau ka aroturukihia e rātou i ia te wā. Kei te tuku pūrongo te rōpū whakahaere kei te whakahaeretia te pokapū i runga o ngā aratohu e ai ki te tahua. Ka tautoko Te Wānanga o Raukawa i ngā kaiwhakahaere ki te aroturuki i tā rātou whakapaunga pūtea me te whakatutuki i o rātou tumanako ā-tahua.

Te ako me te whakawhanaketanga ngaio. Kua whānui te ako me te whakawhanaketanga ngaio mō te katoa o ngā kaimahi. Kua tino arotahi ēnei akoranga ki te whakanui ake i te tōtika o te hōtaka me te whakanui ake i te ōrite o ngā mahi ngaiotanga. Kua puta mai i naiānei tētahi ahurea mahi tahi i waenganui i ngā kaimahi ka whai whakaaro i ia te wā ki te tōtika o te hōtaka. Ka tautoko ngā whāinga ako me te whakawhanaketanga ngaio ā-rōpū ā-tau i ngā kaimahi ki te whakapai ake i ā rātou mahi ngaiotanga. Ka whakapā takatika tēnei ki te kounga o te hōtaka akoranga a ngā tamariki.

Te whakahaere i ngā whanonga o ngā tamariki. I whakapau kaha ngā kaimahi me ngā kaiwhakahaere o te pokapū ki te whakapai ake i ngā rautaki whakahaere whanonga. Kua kaha ake tā rātou aro ki ngā matea whānui o ngā tamariki. Kua arotakehia te kaupapa here me ngā tukanga e pā ana ki te ārahi takatika, ā, kei te hono i nāianei ki te kaupapa me te tirohanga whānui a te pokapū. Kua nui hoki te ako me te whakawhanaketanga ngaio mō ngā kaimahi e pā ana ki te whakahaere whanonga. Nō reira kua puta mai te māramatanga ngātahi ki ngā tūmanakohanga me ngā mahi whakahaere whanonga. He pai te tiaki i ngā tamariki, te whakaute me te poipoi i a rātou. Ka whakautu wawe ngā kaimahi ki ngā matea ā-whatumanawa, ā-tinana o ngā tamariki.

Ngā wāhanga hei whakapai ake anō

Te whakakaha ake i te aromihi kaimahi.Ahakoa kei ngā aromātai whaiaro ā ngā kaimahi ētahi kōrero aromihi, me whai whakaaro tonu ki ētahi atu rautaki kia kaha ake te hōtaka aromihi. Kei te mōhio ngā kaiwhakahaere o te pokapū me ngā kaimahi he wāhanga tēnei hei whakawhanake tonu mā rātou.

Ngā wāhanga kāore i tutuki ki ngā hanganga ture

He whakamārama

I tāutuhia e te pūrongo a te Tari Arotake Mātauranga o Hereturikōka 2010 ngā wāhanga e rua kāore i tutuki ki ngā hanganga ture. Kāore ngā tuhinga e pā ana ki te hoatu rongoā, i te hopu i ngā kōrero katoa e tika ana. Kāore he wāhi tōtika hei tīni i ngā kope o ngā pēpi. Kua whakatikahia e te whānau ngā wāhanga nei.

Ngā wāhanga kua pai ake

Te wāhi tīni kope. Kua whakaritea tētahi wāhi tīni kope kia pai ake ai te tautoko i ngā kaimahi me ngā pepi i ngā wā ka whakahaeretia tēnei āhuatanga.

Te hoatu rongoā. Kua takoto tika ngā kōrero i nāianei e pā ana ki te hoatu rongoā ki ngā tamariki.

1 Background

Introduction

A Supplementary Review is undertaken at the discretion of a National Manager in the Education Review Office (ERO).

A supplementary review evaluates the extent and effectiveness of actions a centre has taken towards addressing issues specified in a previous education review and/or any additional areas identified since that review.

Terms of Reference

This supplementary review is based on an evaluation of the performance of the He Iti Nā Motai governing body and management in relation to areas identified in the August 2010 ERO report or issues identified since th

at review. The terms of reference for this review are to investigate:

  • the quality of education including behaviour management, personnel management, leadership, annual and strategic planning, centre kaupapa and practice and annual budgeting
  • areas of non-compliance including recording the administration of medicines, and providing a suitable place for nappy changing.

2 The Education Review Office (ERO) Evaluation

He Iti Na Motai is a Maori immersion Early Childhood Centre located at Te Wānanga o Raukawa, Otaki. At the time of the 2010 ERO review, the centre was working through a number of organisational and staffing matters which presented challenges to existing management, and staff practice. The centre is now formally recognised as an entity of the parent body, Te Wānanga o Raukawa. Centre management, staff and whānau have worked successfully together to strengthen areas for review and development identified in the previous ERO report. Through this work centre operations and the programme for children have been enhanced.

Centre management members, staff and whānau have worked collectively to achieve a shared understanding of the centre’s philosophy and guiding principles. There is regular discussion among the whānau about the implementation of the kaupapa, programme principles and staff practice.

Since the last ERO review, a centre leader has been appointed. Extensive professional learning and development has been provided to all staff. This has focused on achieving consistent programme management, implementation of the kaupapa and care of children. These areas continue to be monitored closely by the centre manager and management team.

Annual priorities are planned. These link directly to the centre’s strategic vision and direction. Priorities have been identified through an analysis of the collective strengths and needs of centre members. Centre management report that there is a shared effort to monitor and manage the annual budget and expenditure.

ERO recommends that centre members further strengthen formal review and reporting practices across all areas of centre management and operations.

Future Action

ERO is confident that the service is being managed in the interests of the children. Therefore ERO will review the service again as part of the regular review cycle.

3 Findings

The Quality of Education

Background

The August 2010 ERO report identified several areas for development and review. These included the need for staff and whānau to develop a collective understanding of their kaupapa, appointing a professional leader to increase the effectiveness of centre operations, personnel management including performance appraisal and the programme. Closer monitoring of the budget was required. Review of the positive guidance policy to improve behaviour management procedures was another concern that needed to be addressed. Since the last ERO review, centre members have worked hard to progress all areas identified for development.

Areas of progress

Integrating the kaupapa and philosophy into centre practice.The kaupapa and philosophy of the centre is now understood by centre members and whānau. Much work has been completed by staff and management to integrate their kaupapa into centre practices. Staff have undertaken significant professional learning and development to achieve this. Programme planning, delivery and assessment have been included in professional development and learning. Staff reflection about these practices is ongoing and contributes to programme development. Whānau members are regularly informed about kaupapa and philosophy through whānau pānui, and children’s development portfolios.

Priority planning.Annual priorities are planned and link to the centre’s strategic plan. Annual priority planning is particularly strong for staff development.

Leadership and direction.Since the last ERO review, the management team has appointed a manager to guide centre operations, personnel management and programme development. This has had a positive impact on personnel management and the children’s learning programme.

Expenditure.Centre managers have an annual budget which they regularly monitor. The management team report that the centre operates within budget guidelines. Te Wānanga o Raukawa supports management to monitor their expenditure and to achieve budget expectations.

Professional learning and development. Professional learning and development (PLD) for all centre staff has been extensive. It has largely focused on increasing programme effectiveness and increasing professional practice consistency. There is now a cohesive work culture between staff who regularly reflect on the effectiveness of the programme. The 2011 annual group PLD goals support kaimahi to improve their professional practice. This impacts positively on the quality of the children’s learning programme.

Managing children’s behaviour.Staff and centre management have worked hard to improve behaviour management strategies. They are now more responsive to the diverse needs of children. The positive guidance policy and procedures have been reviewed and now link with the centre’s kaupapa and philosophy. Staff PLD for behaviour management has been significant. This has brought about a shared understanding of behaviour management expectations and practices. Children are well cared for, respected and nurtured. Kaimahi respond promptly to the emotional and physical needs of children.

Area for further improvement

Strengthening performance appraisal.While staff self evaluations provide a source of appraisal information other sources or methods could be considered to bring about a more robust appraisal programme. Centre managers and staff recognise that appraisal systems require further development.

Areas of Non-Compliance

Background

The August 2010 ERO report identified two areas of non-compliance. Records of medicines administered to children lacked all necessary information. There was no suitable nappy changing area. Members have addressed both areas of non-compliance.

Areas of progress

Nappy facilities.A suitable nappy changing area is now in place to better support kaimahi and babies during nappy changing.

Administering medicines.Appropriate details are now recorded when medicine is administered.

4 Recommendations

  • centre members further develop formal review and reporting across all areas of centre management and operations.

5 Future Action

ERO is confident that the service is being managed in the interests of the children. Therefore ERO will review the service again as part of the regular review cycle.
