1 He Whakamārama

He Kupu Whakataki

Ka whakahaeretia tētahi arotake tāpiri i runga o te whakataunga ā te Kaiurungi Whakaturuki Arotake Māori i Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga.

E aromātai ana te arotake tāpiri i te whānuitanga me te whai hua o ngā mahi ā te kōhanga reo ki te whakatutuki i ngā take i whakatakotohia ai i roto i te arotake mātauranga o mua atu i tēnei, me ētahi atu wāhanga rānei kua tāutuhia whai muri o taua arotake.

Ngā Whakataunga mō ngā Whakapuakitanga

Ko tēnei arotake tāpiri i whakaritea i runga o te aromātaitanga o te whakatutukitanga a te rōpū kāwanatanga, whakahaeretanga o te kōhanga reo, e pā ana ki ngā wāhanga i tāutuhia ai e te pūrongo a Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga, i mua atu, me ngā take rānei kua tāutuhia whai muri o taua arotake. Ko ngā whakataunga mō ngā whakapuakitanga e pā ana ki tēnei arotake, ko te titiro ki:

  • te whakamahere me te aromātai, tae atu ki te whakahaeretanga a te whānau, te whakamahere, te aromatawai, te arotake kaupapa here, ngā kaimahi, te whakawhanaketanga ngaio, te whakamahere rautaki, ngā arohaehae, me te arotake whaiaro
  • te whakarato i ngā rauemi hei hāpai i ngā akoranga a ngā tamariki
  • ētahi atu take i tāutuhia ai whai muri i te arotake o mua.

2 Te Horopaki

E tū ana Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Tīmatatanga Hou ki Te Puke. Nō te tau 1992 te kōhanga reo i whakatūngia ai e ngā mema o tētahi whare karakia o konei.

Ka tāutu te pūrongo o mua a Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga, i te marama o Mahuru, i te tau 2013 i te maha o ngā wāhanga hei whakawhanake ake. I taua wā, me whakamārama te whānau i ō rātou tūranga, ā rātou kawenga mahi hoki, ā, kāhore te arotake whaiaro e tino whakamahia ana hei rautaki mō te whakapai haere. Mai i taua wā, kua whakatūngia tētahi kaiwhakahaere hou me tētahi kaimahi hou.

3 Ngā Whakaaturanga

Ngā rauemi hei hāpai i te ako a ngā tamariki

Ngā wāhanga e pai ana te whakahaere

E wātea ana ki ngā tamariki i raro i te rua o ngā tau te maha o ngā tūmomo rauemi e tika ana, e whakatutuki ana hoki i ō rātou tūmomo āheinga me ō rātou ngākau nuitanga. E tino tautokona ana te ako o ngā tamariki i raro i te rua o ngā tau.

Te whakamahere me te aromātai

Ngā wāhanga e pai ana te whakahaere

Ka whiwhi ngā tamariki i ngā hōtaka akoranga i āta whakamaheretia ai. Ka āta ārahi ngā mahere i te whakapuakitanga o te hōtaka akoranga. Ka whakamahi ngā kaimahi i ngā tino tukanga ki te aromatawai i ngā akoranga a ngā tamariki i ia te wā. E āhei ana te whānau ki te pānui i aua mōhiohio me te whakatakoto i ngā kōrero urupare. Ka whai hua ngā tamariki nā ā rātou hōtaka akoranga.

Kua whakatūngia e te whānau tētahi kaiwhakahaere hou ka whakahaere i ngā mahi me ngā whakahaeretanga o ia rā. He māramatanga ngātahi tō te whānau me ngā kaimahi mō ō rātou tūranga, me ā rātou kawenga mahi. He matawhānui ngā kaupapa here me ngā tukanga, ā, e whai kiko ana hoki ēnei hei ārahi i a rātou.

Kei te whānau ētahi tukanga arotake e whai hua ana. Kua whakahou ake rātou i te mahere rautaki, tae atu ki ngā whāinga rautaki, ngā mahi, me te mahere ā-tau. Ko te ako me te waiora o ngā tamariki te aronga matua o te whānau.

Kua pai ake te whakahaere i ngā kaimahi. He tuhinga tūranga mahi ā ngā kaimahi hou. Ka whakarato te whānau i ngā whai wāhitanga mō ngā kaimahi ki te uru atu ki te whakawhanaketanga ngaio. Ka tautoko ngā kaupapa kaimahi o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo i ngā kaimahi ki te whakapai ake i ā rātou whakaritenga. Ka whai pūtake te hōtaka akoranga a ngā tamariki.

Ngā Whakaritenga Matua ka Whai Ake

Kāhore te whānau e whakatutuki ana i ia te wā i ngā arowhai e pā ana ki te hauora me te haumaru. I te wā o te arotake, he maha ngā āhuatanga mōrearea i tāutuhia ai, puta noa i te wāhi o roto, o waho hoki. Kua roa nei te wā kāhore te whānau e whakamahi ana i tā rātou rārangi arowhai mōrearea. Ko te roanga o ngā mahi whakatikatika mō te wāhi tākaro o waho, e aukati ana i te āheinga o ngā tamariki ki te tākaro ki tēnei wāhi. Kua whakakōroiroi te tūhura o ngā tamariki ki tō rātou taiao.

4 Hei tutuki i ngā herenga ā-ture

Te tirohanga whānui

Hei whakatutuki te whānau o te kōhanga reo i āna herenga ā-ture, me tahuri rātou ki te:

  1. whakarite i te whakatinanatanga o tētahi pūnaha auau e pā ana ki te arohaehae [R47 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; Paearu Raihana GMA7]
  2. whakatutuki i ia te wā i ngā arowhai hauora, haumaru hoki, puta noa i te taiao. [R 46 (1), (2) Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; Paearu Raihana HS12]

5 Ngā Taunakitanga

I whakawhanakehia e Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga me te whānau ngā taunakitanga e whai ake nei, arā, kia

  1. arotake i te mahere rautaki hei āta whakarite i te māramatanga o ngā mahi rautaki
  2. whakapai ake i te arowhai o ngā āhuatanga mōrearea me te whakatika haere.

Te Whakarāpopototanga

E tū ana Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Tīmatatanga Hou ki Te Puke. Ka whiwhi ngā tamariki i ngā hōtaka akoranga i āta whakamaheretia ai, ā, he whai wāhitanga anō hoki mō rātou ki te whai i ō rātou ake ngākau nuitanga, me te whakatau i ā rātou ake whiringa. Ka aromatawaihia tā rātou ahu whakamua i ia te wā. He māramatanga ngātahi tō te whānau me ngā kaimahi ki ō rātou tūranga me ā rātou kawenga mahi. Me whakapai ake i te arowhai o ngā āhuatanga mōrearea me te whakatika haere.

Hei ā hea Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga arotake anō ai i te kōhanga reo?

Ka arotake anō Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga i Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Tīmatatanga Hou i roto i te toru o ngā tau.

1 Background

Introduction

A supplementary review is undertaken at the discretion of the Deputy Chief Review Officer Māori in the Education Review Office (ERO).

A supplementary review evaluates the extent and effectiveness of actions a kōhanga reo has taken towards addressing issues specified in its previous ERO review and/or any additional areas identified since that review.

Terms of Reference

This supplementary review is based on an evaluation of the performance of the kōhanga reo governing body and management in relation to areas identified in the previous ERO report or issues identified since that review. The terms of reference for this review are to investigate:

  • planning and evaluation including whānau management, planning, assessment, policy review, personnel, professional development, strategic planning, appraisals, self review
  • resourcing to support children’s learning
  • any issues identified since the previous review.

2 Context

Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Tīmatatanga Hou is in Te Puke. The kōhanga reo was established in 1992 by members of a local church.

The previous ERO report of September 2013, identified several areas that required development. Whānau needed to clarify their roles and responsibilities and self-review needed was underutilised as a tool for improvement. Since then a new kaiwhakahaere and kaimahi have been employed.

3 Findings

Resourcing to support children’s learning

Areas of progress

The children under the age of two have access to various resources that are appropriate and cater well for their different abilities and interests. The under two year olds are well supported in their learning.

Planning and Evaluation

Areas of progress

Children experience well planned learning programmes. Planning effectively guides the delivery of the learning programme. Kaimahi use sound processes to regularly assess children’s learning. Whānau are able to view this information and provide feedback. Children benefit from their learning programmes.

Whānau have appointed a new kaiwhakahaere to manage day-to-day tasks and operations. Whānau and kaimahi have a shared understanding of their roles and responsibilities. There are comprehensive and detailed policies and procedures to guide them.

Whānau have some useful review processes in place. They have renewed the strategic plan which includes strategic objectives, actions and annual plan. Children’s learning and wellbeing are the primary focus for whānau.

Personnel management has improved. New kaimahi have job descriptions. Whānau provide opportunities for kaimahi to engage in professional development. Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust district trainers support kaimahi to improve their practice. Children have a purposeful learning programme.

Key next steps

Whānau do not regularly conduct health and safety checks. During the review, numerous hazards were identified throughout the indoor and outdoor areas. Whānau have not used their hazards checklist for awhile. Long term repair to the outside play area hinders access for children to this space. Children’s ability to explore their environment is limited.

4 Actions of non-compliance

Overview

In order for the kōhanga reo whānau to meet compliance requirements they need to:

  • ensure that a system of regular appraisal is implemented [R47 Education (ECS) Regulations 2008 GMA7]
  • regularly conduct health and safety checks throughout the environment. [R46 (1), (2) ECS2008/HS12) Regulations 2008 HS12]

5 Recommendations

ERO and the whānau developed the following recommendations:

  1. review the strategic plan to ensure that strategic actions are clear
  2. improve hazard checking and maintenance.

Conclusion

Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Tīmatatanga Hou is in Te Puke. Children experience well planned learning programmes and have opportunities to follow their interests and make their own choices. Their progress is regularly assessed. Whānau and kaiako have a shared understanding of their roles and responsibilities. Hazard checking and maintenance requires further improvement. 

When is ERO likely to review the kōhanga reo again?

The next ERO review of Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Tīmatatanga Hou will be in three years.
