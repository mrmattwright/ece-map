1 Te Aromātai a te Tari Arotake Mātauranga

Ko Mātaatua te waka

Ko Urukaraka te maunga

Ko Ōhinemataroa te awa

Ko Ngāti Tāwhaki te hapū

Ko Ngāi Tūhoe te iwi

Moumou kai, moumou tangata ki te pō 

E tū ana Te Kōhanga Reo o Tāwhaki ki te marae o Ngāhina.  Ko te tokomaha o te whānau kua noho ki te kōhanga reo, mai i tōna tīmatanga. He pai te tūnga o te kōhanga reo ki tōna hapori. Koia nei te kōhanga reo rahi rawa atu o Rūātoki.  E ai ki te whānau, ko te marae, te Hāhi Ringatū, me te tū hei Tūhoe e noho pūmau ana ki ngā whakaakoranga o te kōhanga reo. I whakaritea e te whānau ko Ngā Taumata Whakahirahira te kaupapa arotahi mō tēnei arotake. E whakapono ana rātou, ko ngā mōhiotanga e pā ana ki te Tūhoetanga, ka kawea e te reo o Tūhoe. Ka aromātai tēnei arotake i te whānuitanga o te whānau me ngā kaimahi ki te whakatairanga me te poipoi i te waiora ā-whatumanawa o ngā tamariki, me tō rātou Tūhoetanga. 

He pai te whakahaeretanga, te whakaritenga hoki o te hōtaka akoranga. Ka whakarato ki ngā tamariki ngā wheako akoranga e whakatairanga, e whakanui ana hoki i tō rātou waiora me tō rātou tuakiri hei Tūhoe. Ka rumakina ngā tamariki ki te reo Māori me ngā tikanga o Tūhoe. Ka tino mōhio ngā tamariki ko wai rātou, nō hea rātou. Ka kitea tō rātou tino māramatanga ki ngā kawa me ngā tikanga, i a rātou e whakatau manuhiri ana ki tō rātou kōhanga reo. Ka whai pānga nui te Hāhi Ringatū ki te hapori me te kōhanga reo. Ka whai atu ngā tamariki i ngā tūmomo tikanga e hāngai ana ki te Hāhi Ringatū. He harikoa, he mauritau hoki ngā tamariki i te kōhanga reo. Ka whakarato ngā kaimahi i ngā whai wāhitanga mō ngā tamariki ki te tūhura i te kōhanga reo, te marae, me te ao whānui. Ka poipoia te oranga ā-tinana, ā-hinengaro, ā-tangata hoki o ngā tamariki, nā te whakaratonga o tētahi hōtaka i āta whakaritea ai. 

Ka whakamahi te whānau i ngā tino tukanga ki te whakahaere i te kōhanga reo hei painga mō ā rātou tamariki. Ka noho pūmau te Tūhoetanga ki ngā wāhanga maha o te whakahaeretanga. He matawhānui te whakamahere, te aromatawai, me ngā aromātai hōtaka. Ka noho mārama te whānau ki tēnā e ākona ana e ngā tamariki, ki tā rātou ahunga whakamua anō hoki. Kua arotakengia te tūtohinga, ngā kaupapa here, me ngā tukanga. Kua āta whakaraupapahia ngā wawata o te whānau mō ā rātou tamariki, ki te tūtohinga. He tino aratohu taua tuhinga hei ārahi i ngā whakaritenga. Ka noho matua te hauora me te haumaru o ngā tamariki. Ka whakaritea ngā kaupapa matua a te whānau i raro i Te Ara Tūāpae. Ka whakatutuki te mahere rautaki i te hiahia ki te whakapakari i te reo o Tūhoe mō ngā tamariki me te whānau. He pakari te tūnga pūtea o te kōhanga reo. He rawaka hoki te pūtea mō ngā rauemi mā ngā tamariki. He auau, he mārama hoki ngā pūrongo ki te whānau. 

Me whakapakari te whakahaere i ngā kaimahi. Me arotake, me waitohu hoki e ngā kaimahi ngā tuhinga katoa e pā ana ki ngā tūranga mahi.

2 Ngā Kaupapa Motuhake o te Arotake

Te Arotahi o te Arotake

I mua o te arotake, i īnoitia te whānau kia whai whakaaro ki ā rātou kaupapa motuhake mō te arotake, ā, kia whakamahia ngā aratohu me ngā rauemi i whakaratohia e te Tari Arotake Mātauranga.

Kātahi ka whakaritea ngā tino kaupapa motuhake mō te arotake, whai muri o te whakawhitiwhitinga kōrero i waenga o te rōpū arotake o te Tari Arotake Mātauranga me te whānau.  I arotahi tēnei whakawhitiwhitinga ki ngā kōrero i te puritia e te kōhanga reo (tāpiri atu ki ngā kōrero arotake whaiaro) ā, me te whānui o te pānga o ngā take mō te arotake ki ngā hua ka puta ki ngā tamariki o te kōhanga reo. 

I whakaritea e te whānau ko tāna kaupapa arotahi ko:

  • Ngā Taumata Whakahirahira 

Ko te katoa o ngā arotake mātauranga ā te Tari Arotake Mātauranga i roto o ngā kōhanga reo e arotahi ana ki te kounga o te mātauranga.  Ki tā te Tari Arotake Mātauranga, kei roto i tēnei ko te kounga o te:

  • arotake whaiaro a te whānau me te whakamahere rautaki 
  • whakamahere hōtaka, te aromatawai, me te aromātai. 

Ko ngā kitenga ā te Tari Arotake Mātauranga i roto i ēnei wāhanga, kei raro iho nei.

3 Ngā Whakaaturanga

Ngā Taumata Whakahirahira

He whakamārama

He pēhea te whai huatanga o te whānau me ngā kaimahi ki te whakatairanga me te poipoi i te waiora ā-whatumanawa o ngā tamariki, me tō rātou Tūhoetanga?

Ka tino whai hua te whānau me ngā kaimahi ki te whakatairanga me te poipoi i te waiora ā-whatumanawa o ngā tamariki, me tō rātou Tūhoetanga. 

Ngā wāhanga e pai ana te whakahaere

He pai te whakahaere me te whakaritenga o te hōtaka akoranga. Ka whakaratohia ki ngā tamariki ngā wheako akoranga e whakatairanga ana, e whakanui ana hoki i te whakawhanaketanga o tō rātou waiora me tō rātou tuakiri hei Tūhoe. Ka kitea ki ngā tūmomo āhuatanga e whai ake nei:

Ka rumakina ngā tamariki ki te reo Māori me ngā tikanga o Tūhoe. He māhorahora te whakamahinga o te reo Māori, kia rongo ai ngā tamariki i ngā wā katoa i te kounga o te reo Māori puta noa i te whānuitanga o ngā tūmomo akoranga. 

He pakari ngā tamariki nā runga i te mōhio ko wai rātou, nō hea rātou. Ka whakamahi ngā kaimahi i te whānuitanga o ngā rautaki hei whakatairanga i te māramatanga o ngā tamariki ki tō rātou whakapapa, ki tō rātou tūrangawaewae hoki.  I ia rā, wheako ai ngā tamariki i te Tūhoetanga ki te kōhanga reo. 

He pakari ngā tamariki ki te whānuitanga o tō rātou kōhanga reo, ki te marae, ā, i ngā wā hoki ka haereere rātou ki ngā wāhi whakahirahira o Tūhoe. Ka kitea tō rātou tino māramatanga ki te kawa me ngā tikanga, i a rātou e whakatau manuhiri ana ki tō rātou kōhanga reo. 

He mātātoa te whai wāhi atu a ngā tamariki ki ngā karakia, ngā waiata me ngā mōteatea e hāngai ana ki Tūhoe me te Hāhi Ringatū. Ka whai pānga nui te Hāhi Ringatū ki te hapori me te kōhanga reo. Ka ū tonu ngā tamariki ki ngā tikanga i roto i ā rātou mahinga. Ka poipoia te wairua o te tamaiti nā te akiaki me te whakamihi. He harikoa , he mauritau hoki ngā tamariki. 

Ko te waiora o te ngā tamariki tētahi aronga matua. Ka poipoia te oranga ā-tinana o ngā tamariki nā te whakarato i ngā kai hauora.  E āhei ana ngā tamariki ki te whiriwhiri i ngā taumahi me ngā rauemi e whai pūtake ana ki a rātou.  Ka poipoia te oranga ā-tinana, ā-hinengaro, ā-wairua, ā-tangata hoki o ngā tamariki nā te whakaratonga o tētahi hōtaka i āta whakaritea ai. 

Te Whakamahere me te Aromātai

He whakamārama

He pēhea te whānuitanga o te whānau ki te whakahaere i ngā whakaritenga i te kōhanga reo, me te whakaraupapa haere kia noho matua ai te reo Māori me ngā tikanga o Tūhoe?

Kua whakaritea e te whānau ngā tino tukanga ki te whakahaere i te kōhanga reo hei painga mō ā rātou tamariki. Ka āta kitea te Tūhoetanga ki ngā kaupapa here, ngā tukanga, me ngā whakaritenga. 

Ngā wāhanga e pai ana te whakahaere

Te whakamahere, te aromātai, me te aromatawai. He matawhānui te whakamahere, te aromatawai, me te aromātai hōtaka. Ka hāngai te kiko o te hōtaka ki ngā mōhiotanga o te hapori. Ka kitea anō hoki aua āhuatanga ki te pūkete a ia tamaiti. Ka hāngai ngā aromātai ki te whakapaitanga ake. Ka noho mārama te whānau ki tēnā i ākona ai e ngā tamariki, me tā rātou ahunga whakamua. 

Te tūtohinga me ngā kaupapa here. E whakarato ana te tūtohinga i ngā aratohu mārama mō ngā whakaritenga. Kua arotakengia te tūtohinga, ngā kaupapa here, me ngā tukanga. Kua āta whakaraupapahia ngā wawata o te whānau mō ā rātou tamariki, ki te tūtohinga. Ka noho matua hoki ki te whānau, ko te hauora me te haumaru o ngā tamariki. 

Te whakamahere rautaki. Ka whai hua te whakamahere rautaki hei ārahi i ngā whakawhanaketanga ka whai ake ā meāke nei. Kua whakaritea ngā kaupapa matua a te kōhanga reo i raro i Te Ara Tūāpae. Ka hāngai te mahere rautaki ki te hiahia ki te whakapakari i te reo o Tūhoe mō ngā tamariki me te whānau. Ka hāpai taua aronga i ngā mahi tauatanga, hei āta whakarite i te noho pūmau o te reo o Tūhoe. 

Te whakahaere i ngā pūtea. He pai te tūnga pūtea o te kōhanga reo. He rawaka te pūtea mō ngā rauemi a ngā tamariki. He auau, he mārama hoki ngā pūrongo ki te whānau. 

Te wāhanga hei whakawhanake

Te whakahaere i ngā kaimahi. Me whakapakari te whakahaere i ngā kaimahi. Kāhore anō ngā tuhinga tūranga mahi, ngā kirimana tuku mahi, me ngā arohaehae kaimahi kia arotakengia, kia whakatinanahia rānei. Ka hāpai aua mahi i te whānau ki te whakatinana i ngā whakaritenga a te kaituku mahi pai, tika anō hoki.

4 Te Whakatau a te Whānau ki ngā Wāhanga Tautukunga

Te Tirohanga Whānui

I mua o te arotake, i whakakīia e te kaipupuri raihana, te whānau, me ngā kaimahi o te kōhanga reo tētahi Tauāki Kupu Tūturu a te Kōhanga Reo kua Raihanatia, o Pipiri 2013, me tētahi Rārangi Tātari Whaiaro mā te Tari Arotake Mātauranga.  I roto i ēnei tuhinga i oati rātou, i whāia e rātou ngā huarahi whai take hei whakatutuki i ā rātou herenga ā-ture e pā ana ki:

  • ngā whakahaerenga ā te whānau;
  • te hauora, te waiora me te whai oranga;
  • ngā whakahaerenga kaimahi;
  • ngā whakahaerenga pūtea me ngā whakahaerenga rawa.

I te wā o te arotake, i whakamātauhia e te Tari Arotake Mātauranga ngā āhuatanga i raro iho nei, i te mea he nui te pānga o ēnei ki ngā hua ka puta ki ngā tamariki:

  • te haumaru whatumanawa (tāpiri atu ko te whakatau tamariki, ko te ārai i ngā mahi whakaweti, mahi tūkino);
  • te haumaru ā-tinana (tāpiri atu ko te whakatau tamariki; ko ngā whakaritenga whakamoe, tiaki tamariki; ko ngā aituā me te whāngai rongoā; ko ngā whakaritenga akuaku; ko ngā kaupapa here me ngā tikanga haerenga whakawaho);
  • ngā tohu mātauranga me ngā whakaritenga kaimahi; me
  • ngā whakaritenga, tikanga hōneatanga e pā ana ki te ahi me te rū whenua.

5 Ngā Taunakitanga

E taunaki ana te Tari Arotake Mātauranga kia arotake, kia whakahou ake e te whānau i ngā tuhinga tūranga mahi me ngā kirimana tuku mahi hoki mā ngā kaimahi, me te whakarite, te whakatinana hoki i te pūnaha arohaehae. 

6 Hei ā hea te Tari Arotake Mātauranga arotake anō a i te kōhanga reo?

Tērā ka whakahaeretia e te Tari Arotake Mātauranga te arotake whai muri, i roto i ngā tau e toru. 

Lynda Pura Watson
Kaiwhakahaere ā-Motu mō ngā Ratonga Arotake Māori (Te Uepū ā-Motu)

1 The Education Review Office (ERO) Evaluation

Ko Mātaatua te waka

Ko Urukaraka te maunga

Ko Ōhinemataroa te awa

Ko Ngāti Tāwhaki te hapu

Ko Ngai Tūhoe te iwi

Moumou kai, moumou tangata ki te pō.

 

Te Kōhanga Reo o Tāwhaki is located on Ngāhina Marae.  Many of the whānau have been at the kōhanga reo since it was established. The kōhanga reo has a good reputation in the community. It is the largest kōhanga reo in Ruātoki. The whānau state that the marae, Te Hāhi Ringatū and being Tūhoe is central to the teachings of the kōhanga reo.  The whānau chose ngā taumata whakahirahira as the focus for this review.  They believe that the language of Tūhoe is the vehicle through which knowledge about Tūhoetanga is transmitted. This review evaluates the extent in which the whānau and kaimahi promote and nurture children’s emotional wellbeing and their Tūhoetanga.

The learning programme is well managed and organised. The children are provided with learning experiences that enhance and promote their wellbeing and identity as Tūhoe. Children are immersed in the language and customs of Tūhoe. Children are confident about who they are and where they come from. They demonstrate a sound understanding of kawa and tikanga when welcoming visitors to their kōhanga reo. Te Hāhi Ringatū is significant in the community and kōhanga reo. Children observe various practices related to the Ringatū faith. Children are happy and positive at kōhanga reo. Kaimahi provide opportunities for children to explore the kōhanga reo, marae and the wider world. Children’s physical, intellectual, spiritual and social wellbeing are nurtured through the provision of a well organised programme.

The whānau use robust processes to manage the kōhanga reo in the best interest of their children. Tūhoetanga remains strong in many areas of operations. Planning, assessment and programme evaluations are comprehensive. Whānau are clear about what children are learning and their progress. The charter, policies and procedures have been reviewed. Whānau aspirations for their children are clearly outlined in the charter. This provides clear guidelines for practice. Children’s health and safety are priorities. The whānau priorities are organised under Te Ara Tūāpae. The strategic plan addresses the need to strengthen te reo o Tūhoe for children and whānau. The kōhanga reo is financially viable. There is an adequate budget for children’s resources. Reporting to whānau is regular and transparent.

Personnel management needs strengthening. All employment documentation needs to be reviewed and signed by kaimahi. 

2 Review Priorities

The Focus of the Review

Before the review, the whānau were invited to consider its priorities for review using guidelines and resources provided by ERO.

The detailed priorities for review were then determined following a discussion between the ERO review team and whānau. This discussion focused on existing information held by the kōhanga reo (including self-review information) and the extent to which potential issues for review contributed to positive outcomes for children at the kōhanga reo.

The whānau chose as its focus area:

  • Ngā Taumata Whakahirahira

All ERO education reviews in kōhanga reo focus on the quality of education. For ERO this includes the quality of:

  • whānau self review and strategic planning
  • programme planning, assessment and evaluation.

ERO’s findings in these areas are set out below.

3 Findings

Ngā Taumata Whakahirahira

Background

How effectively do the whānau and kaimahi promote and nurture children’s emotional wellbeing and their Tūhoetanga?

Whānau and kaimahi effectively promote and nurture children’s emotional wellbeing and their Tūhoetanga.

Areas of good performance

The learning programme is well managed and organised. The children are provided with learning experiences that enhance and promote their wellbeing and identity as Tūhoe. This is evident in the following ways:

Children are immersed in the language and customs of Tūhoe. Te reo Māori is normalised allowing children to constantly hear quality models of te reo Māori across a range of learning situations.

Children are confident in who they are and where they come from. Kaimahi use a range of strategies to enhance children’s understanding of their whakapapa and tūrangawaewae. Children experience Tūhoetanga on a daily basis in the kōhanga reo.

Children are confident in their kōhanga reo surroundings, the marae and when they visit places of significance to Tūhoe. They demonstrate a sound understanding of kawa and tikanga when welcoming visitors to their kōhanga reo.

Children are active participants in karakia, waiata and mōteatea related to Tūhoe and the Ringātu faith. Te Hāhi Ringātu is significant in the community and kōhanga reo. During routines children observe tikanga. Children’s wairua is nurtured through encouragement and praise. Children are happy and positive.

Children’s wellbeing is a priority. The physical wellbeing of children is nurtured through the provision of healthy meals. Children are able to choose activities and resources that have meaning for them. Children’s physical, intellectual, spiritual and social wellbeing are nurtured through the provision of a well organised programme.

Planning and Evaluation

Background

To what extent do whānau manage the kōhanga reo operations and prioritise te reo Māori and ngā tikanga of Tūhoe?

The whānau have in place processes to manage the kōhanga reo in the best interest of their children. Tūhoetanga is strongly evident in policy, procedure and practice.

Areas of good performance

Planning, assessment and programme evaluations. Planning, assessment and programme evaluations are comprehensive. Programme content reflects local knowledge. These are reflected in children’s profiles. Evaluations focus on improvement. Whānau are clear about what children learn and their progress.

The charter and policies. The charter provides clear guidelines for practice. The charter, policies and procedures have been reviewed. Whānau aspirations for their children are in the charter.  Children’s health and safety is paramount for whānau.

Strategic planning. Strategic planning is useful for guiding future developments. The kōhanga reo priorities are organised under Te Ara Tūāpae. The strategic plan addresses the need to strengthen te reo o Tūhoe for children and whānau. This approach supports succession planning and makes sure te reo o Tūhoe remains strong.

Financial management. The kōhanga reo is financially viable. There is an adequate budget for children’s resources. Reporting to whānau is regular and transparent.

Area for development

Personnel management. Personnel management needs strengthening. Job descriptions, employment contracts and kaimahi appraisals have not been reviewed or implemented. This will assist the whānau implement good, fair employer practices.

4 Whānau Management Assurance on Compliance Areas

Overview

Before the review, the licensee, whānau and staff of the kōhanga reo completed an ERO Licensed Kōhanga Reo Assurance Statement June 2013 and Self-Audit Checklist. In these documents they have attested that they had taken all reasonable steps to meet their legislative obligations related to:

  • administration
  • health, safety and welfare
  • personnel management
  • financial and property management.

During the review, ERO checked the following items because they have a potentially high impact on outcomes for children:

  • emotional safety (including behaviour management, prevention of bullying and abuse)
  • physical safety (including behaviour management, sleeping and supervision practices; accidents and medication; hygiene and routines; travel and excursion policies and procedures)
  • staff qualifications and organisation
  • evacuation procedures and practices for fire and earthquake.

5 Recommendations

ERO recommends the whānau review and update kaimahi job descriptions, employment contracts and establish and implement the appraisal system.

6 When is ERO likely to review the kōhanga reo again?

ERO is likely to carry out the next review in three years.
