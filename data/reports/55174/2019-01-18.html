1 He Whakamārama

He Kupu Whakataki

Ka whakahaeretia tētahi aromātai tāpiri i runga o te whakataunga ā te Toihau Arotake i Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga.

E aromātai ana Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga i te whānuitanga me te whai hua o ngā mahi ā te kōhanga reo ki te whakatutuki i ngā take i whakatakotohia ai i roto i te pūrongo aromātai mātauranga o mua atu i tēnei, me ētahi atu wāhanga rānei i tāutuhia ai whai muri o taua aromātai.

Ngā Whakataunga mō ngā Whakapuakitanga

Ko tēnei aromātai tāpiri i whakaritea i runga i te aromātai o te whakatutukitanga a te whānau i te whakahaeretanga o te kōhanga reo, ki ngā wāhanga i tāutuhia ai e te pūrongo a Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga, i mua atu, me ngā take rānei kua tāutuhia whai muri o taua aromātai. Ko ngā whakataunga mō ngā whakapuakitanga e pā ana ki tēnei aromātai, ko te titiro ki:

  • te hōtaka akoranga – tae atu ki te whakaratonga o ngā rauemi
  • te whakamahere me te aromātai – tae atu ki te whakamahere i te hōtaka, te aromatawai, me te aromātai, te arotake i te tūtohinga me ngā kaupapa here, te whakamahere rautaki, te whakamahere ā-tau, me te whakahaere i te tari o te kōhanga reo me ngā whakaritenga
  • ngā wāhanga kāhore e tutuki ana e ai ki te ture – tae atu ki te marau, te whakahaere i ngā āhuatanga mōrearea, te whāngai i ngā rongoā, ngā tukanga e pā ana ki ngā hōneatanga ohorere, me ngā herenga ā-ture e pā ana ki ngā whare
  • ētahi atu take i tāutuhia ai whai muri i te arotake o mua.

2 Te Horopaki

E tū ana Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Tihi o Tītīrangi ki te hapori o Kaitī, ki Tūranga-nui-a-Kiwa. I tāutu te arotake o mua a Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga i ngā tino āhuatanga maha o ngā mahi whakahaere a te whānau hei whakapai ake. No muri i te arotake o mua, kua tino panoni haere ngā kaimahi me te whānau. Kua whakatutukihia e te whānau ngā wāhanga maha o ngā āhuatanga hei whakapai ake.

3 Ngā Whakaaturanga

E tino poipoia ana ngā tamariki hei ākonga. E tino whakahaerehia ana te hōtaka akoranga e ngā kaimahi. Kua waia ngā tamariki ki ngā mahinga, ā, he ngāwari te rere o ngā whakawhitinga. Ka whakarato ngā kaimahi i te whānuitanga o ngā tūmomo taumahi, ngā rauemi, me ngā wheako. Kei te whakahihiri, kei te whai wāhi nui hoki ngā tamariki hei ākonga. Kei te tino poipoia ngā pēpi. He aroha, he manaakitanga hoki tō ngā taunekeneke. He mauritau te āhua o ngā pēpi. He ngākau tākare tō te kaiako, ā, kei te whitawhita hoki ia. He pakari ia ki te whakawhitiwhiti whakaaro. He ngākau titikaha ō ngā tamariki ki te ako.

He matawhānui ngā mahere hōtaka, ā, e ārahi ana hoki i te whakapuakitanga o te hōtaka akoranga. Kua āta whakaraupapahia te kiko o te hōtaka. He tika ngā rautaki e kōkiri ana i ngā akoranga. He pakari ngā tukanga aromatawai. Ka whakamahi ngā kaimahi i te whānuitanga o ngā tūmomo huarahi aromatawai - pērā i ngā kōnae me ngā Mātai i te Tamaiti - hei tohu i te ako a ngā tamariki me tō rātou whakawhanaketanga i roto i te roanga o te wā. He pai te whakamōhio i te whānau mō te ako a ngā tamariki me tā rātou ahu whakamua.

Kua pai ake ngā whakaritenga e pā ana ki ngā mahi whakahaere a te whānau. E whakawhanakehia ana ngā mahi e arotake ana i te tūtohinga. E ārahi ana tēnei i ngā mahi whakahaere a te whānau me ngā whakaritenga o te kōhanga reo. Kua whakatakotohia ngā mahere rautaki, ā, kei te mārama ki te whānau tā rātou ahu whakamua mō meāke nei. Kua tāutuhia e rātou ngā kaupapa matua me ngā whāinga e pā ana ki te kōhanga reo. Kua whakaritea tētahi mahere ā-tau, ā, e ārahi ana i ngā wāhanga matua o ngā mahi whakahaere a te whānau. I whakatūngia e te whānau tētahi kaiwhakahaere, whai muri i te arotake o mua. Kua aro atu te whānau ki te whakapai ake i ngā pūnaha e whakahaere ana i te tari. Kua whakatakotohia te katoa o ngā rehita e pā ana ki te hauora me te haumaru. I te wā o tēnei arotake, kāhore he take anō i pupū ake i roto i te whakatutukitanga o ngā herenga ā-ture. E whakahaere ana te whānau i te kōhanga reo hei painga mō ngā tamariki.

Ngā Whakaritenga Matua ka Whai Ake

Tērā ētahi wāhanga o ngā mahi whakahaere a te whānau hei mahi tonu, hei whakatairanga ake i te kounga tonu o te arotake o roto. Me whakawhanake te whānau i tētahi mahere mahi hei whakahāngai i tō rātou whakapau kaha ki te whakatutuki i ngā whāinga i tā rātou mahere rautaki, ā, hei aroturuki, hei pūrongo hoki i tā rātou ahu whakamua. Kua tae ki te wā ki te arotake i ētahi o ngā kaupapa here e pā ana ki te hauora me te haumaru, ā, me whai wāhi ēnei hei āhuatanga tonu o te huringa taiāwhio e arotake ai i ngā kaupapa here. Me whai ārahitanga ngā kaimahi ki te aromātai i te whai huatanga o te hōtaka akoranga.

4 Te Taunakitanga

I whakawhanakehia e Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga me te whānau o te kōhanga reo te taunakitanga e whai ake nei, arā, kia:

  • whakapakarihia te arotake o roto me te aromātai i te hōtaka.

Hei ā hea Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga aromātai anō ai i te kōhanga reo?

Ka whakahaere anō Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga i te aromātai whai muri o Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Tihi o Tītīrangi i roto i te toru o ngā tau.

1 Background

Introduction

A supplementary evaluation is undertaken at the discretion of the Chief Review Officer of the Education Review Office (ERO).

ERO evaluates the extent and effectiveness of actions a kōhanga reo has taken towards addressing issues specified in its previous ERO evaluation and/or any additional areas identified since that evaluation.

Terms of Reference

This supplementary evaluation is based on an evaluation of the performance of the kōhanga reo governing body and management in relation to areas identified in the previous ERO report or issues identified since that evaluation. The terms of reference for this evaluation area to investigate:

  • the learning programme – including provision of resources

  • planning and evaluation – including programme planning, assessment and evaluation, charter and policy review, strategic planning, annual planning and administration of kōhanga reo operations

  • areas of non-compliance – including curriculum, hazard management, administering medication, emergency evacuation procedures, and building compliance

  • any other issues identified since the previous review.

2 Context

Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Tihi o Tītīrangi is located in the suburb of Kaitī, Gisborne. The previous ERO review identified a number of significant areas pertaining to whānau management that required improvement. Since the previous review, there have been major changes in kaimahi and whānau. The whānau have been able to address many of the areas for improvement.

3 Findings

Children are well catered for as learners. The learning programme is well managed by kaimahi. Children are familiar with the routines and the transitions are smooth. Kaimahi provide a range of activities, resources and experiences. Children are interested and engaged learners. Babies are well catered for. Interactions are loving and caring. Babies appear secure and settled. The kaiako is enthusiastic with high energy levels. She is a clear communicator. Children are confident learners.

Programme planning is comprehensive and guides the delivery of the learning programme. Content is clearly outlined. The strategies for facilitating learning are appropriate. Sound assessment processes are in place. Kaimahi use a range of assessment methods such as portfolios and Mātai i te Tamaiti to show children’s learning and development over time. The whānau are well informed about children’s learning and progress.

Whānau management practices have improved. Charter review is under development. This provides guidance for whānau in the management and operations of the kōhanga reo. Strategic planning is in place and the whānau are clear about their future direction. They have identified priorities and goals for the kōhanga reo. An annual plan is in place and guides key aspects of whānau management. Since the previous review, the whānau have appointed a kaiwhakahaere. The whānau have worked on improving the administration systems. All health and safety registers are in place. There were no further matters of non-compliance identified during this review. The whānau manage the kōhanga reo in the interests of the children.

Key Next Steps

There are aspects of whānau management that require ongoing work to enhance the overall quality of internal review. The whānau should develop an action plan to focus their efforts towards achieving the goals in their strategic plan, and to monitor and report their progress. Some health and safety policies are due for review and should be included as part of a regular cycle of policy review. Kaimahi require guidance to evaluate the effectiveness of the learning programme.

4 Recommendation

ERO and the kōhanga reo whānau developed the following recommendation:

  • Strengthen internal review and programme evaluation.

When is ERO likely to evaluate the kōhanga reo again?

The next ERO evaluation of Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Tihi o Tītīrangi will be in three years.
