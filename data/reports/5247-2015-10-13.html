Background

St John‚Äôs Hill Kindergarten is situated in Whanganui. It is one of 14 kindergartens administered by the Whanganui Kindergarten Association. The kindergarten is licensed for 40 children. The governing board is responsible for setting the overall strategic direction for the organisation. There has been sustained progress in relation to areas of improvement identified in the October 2012 ERO review. This has been well supported by focused professional learning and development.
