Te Aromātai

1 Te Horopaki

E tū ana Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Maunga Kōhungahunga ki Papakura, ki Tāmaki-makau-rau ki te tonga. I tāutuhia ki te pūrongo o mua a Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga, kua ahu whakamua ngā mema o te whānau ki te whakapai haere i te whakahaeretanga me te āhua o ngā mahi a ngā kaimahi.

I te tau 2015, i kitea e ngā kaupapa kaimahi ā-rohe o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo, kāhore te whānau i whakapūmau i ngā whakapaitanga, ā, me hāpai ake anō i a rātou ki te whakahaere i te kōhanga reo. I wawao atu Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo, ā, kua tīmata ki te whakahāngai anō i te titiro o te whānau ki Te Korowai me ā rātou kawenga e pā ana ki te atawhai me te ako a ngā tamariki. Ko tā Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga arotahi mō tēnei arotake, ko te kounga o te whakahaeretanga ki te hāpai i te waiora o ngā tamariki.

2 Ngā Whakaaturanga o te Arotake

He pēhea rawa te māramatanga o te whānau ki ō rātou tūranga, ā rātou kawenga hoki hei hāpai i ngā tamariki?

He iti noa te māramatanga o te whānau ki ō rātou tūranga me ā rātou kawenga.

Te Korowai

Ko te ngoikore o ngā mahi whakahaere a te whānau ki te kōhanga reo, kua pā tōraro ki ngā akoranga a ngā tamariki. Mō ngā tau e rua kua pahure ake nei, kāhore i te riterite ngā tūnga kaimahi, ā, he kaha rawa te rerekē haere o te whānau. Kāhore ngā tukanga ki te whakauru i ngā whānau hou e whai hua ana, ā, kāhore ngā whānau hou e tino mōhio ki ō rātou tūranga, me ā rātou kawenga. Kāhore he mahere rautaki hei hāpai i te ahu whakamua o te kōhanga reo mō meāke nei. Kāhore ngā whakataunga a te whānau e whai mōhiohio ana, ā, kāhore e arotahi ana ki ngā tamariki. Kei te pānekeneke te tūnga pūtea o te kōhanga reo. Ko te ngoikore o ngā whakawhitinga kōrero me ngā whakaritenga whakahaere i whai pānga anō hoki ki te kaha pā mai o te taupatupatu me te rangirua. Kāhore Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga e noho pūmau ana ki te whakaaro, ka whakawhanake ngā tamariki hei ākonga tūmāia, hei ākonga harikoa, pakari ā-whatumanawa hoki ki tēnei taiao.

Kāhore te tūtohinga e mārama ana, kāhore rānei e whai hua ana. Kāhore te nuinga o ngā kaupapa here me ngā tukanga e hāngai ana ki te wā, ā, kāhore i te rawaka te ārahitanga o roto hei hāpai i te hauora me te haumaru. Kāhore ngā mahi hauora, ngā mahi haumaru hoki a ngā kaimahi e tino whakatutukihia ana. Kāhore te noho haumaru o ngā tamariki e whakapūmautia ana ki te kōhanga reo.

He ngoikore ngā pūnaha whakahaere tari. Kāhore he rauemi i tohaina ai hei hāpai i ngā mahi whakahaere tari, ngā mahi whakahaere kōhanga reo hoki i ia rā. He uaua te toro atu ki ngā tuhinga a te kōhanga reo. Kāhore e taea e ngā mātua te whakapā atu ki te kōhanga reo i ngā haora e whakahaerehia ana, ā, kāhore ngā mahi māmā e whakatutukihia ana.

Kāhore i te rawaka te whakahaeretanga o ngā kaimahi. Kāhore anō ngā kaimahi kia arohaehaengia. Kāhore ngā kaimahi hou e whakamātauhia ana mā Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo, ā, kāhore anō kia oti ngā tukanga e pā ana ki te arowhai a ngā pirihimana i ngā kaimahi. Kāhore ngā mahi whakamahere hōtaka e tino whakawhanakehia ana, puta noa i te kōhanga reo.

Kei te noho āwangawanga te haumaru ā-tinana o ngā tamariki. Kāhore ngā whakahoutanga ki te whare o te kōhanga reo e whakatutuki ana i ngā herenga ā-ture e tika ana. Kāhore he tuhinga kia whakaae te whānau ki te whāngai rongoā. Ko ngā akoako hōneatanga inā he rū whenua, he ahi rānei, ko te arowhai i ngā āhuatanga mōrearea me ngā rawa, kāhore e whakatutukihia ana i ia te wā, kāhore rānei e tuhia ana.

3 Te Whakataunga a te Kōhanga Reo ki ngā Wāhanga Tautukunga

I te wā o te arotake, i whakamātauhia e Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga ngā pūnaha o te kōhanga reo ki te whakahaere i ngā āhuatanga i raro iho nei e whai pānga nui ana ki te waiora o ngā tamariki:

  • te haumaru whatumanawa (tāpiri atu ko te ārahi mauritau, ko te ārai tamariki)
  • te haumaru ā-tinana (tāpiri atu ko te mātakitaki tamariki; ko ngā whakaritenga whakamoe; ko ngā aituā; ko te whāngai rongoā; ko ngā whakaritenga akuaku; ko ngā kaupapa here me ngā tikanga haerenga whakawaho)
  • te tū tika o ngā kaimahi (tae atu ki ngā taumata tohu mātauranga; te arowhai a ngā pirihimana; ngā rēhitatanga kaiako; me te tatauranga ki waenga i te kaiako me te tamaiti)
  • ngā whakaritenga, tikanga hōneatanga e pā ana ki te ahi me te rū whenua.

Ko te tikanga, ka whakatairanga ngā kōhanga reo katoa i te hauora me te haumaru o ngā tamariki, me te arotake anō hoki i ia te wā i tā rātou whakatutukitanga i ngā herenga ā-ture.

I te wā o te arotake, i tāutuhia e Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga ngā wāhanga maha kāhore e tutuki ana e ai ki te ture. Me tahuri te whānau me tā rātou kaipupuri raihana ki te:

  • whakarite i te whakatinanatanga o ngā whakaritenga me ngā tukanga e tika ana mō te whakahaere i ngā pūmanawa o ngā tāngata, ā, me rawaka te tautoko ngaio, ngā whai wāhitanga whakawhanaketanga ngaio hoki i ngā kaimahi me ngā tāngata e whai pānga ana ki te ratonga[R47 (1-2) Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; GMA 7 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]
  • whakarite kia kaua tētahi e whāngai i te rongoā (i tohua ai e te rata, i hokona ai rānei) ki te tamaiti, atu i te rata, i te kaikawe tūroro rānei inā he raru ohorere, i te matua rānei o te tamaiti, i te whakaaetanga ā-tuhi rānei (i runga anō i te āhua tonu o te rongoā) a tētahi matua, ā, mō ngā pākeke ka whāngai rongoā ki ngā tamariki (tua atu i ā rātou ake tamariki), me whiwhi rātou i ngā mōhiohio, i ngā whakangungu rānei e tika ana mō tēnei tūmomo mahi[R46 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; HS 24-30 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]
  • whakarite kia whai hua ai te kāwanatanga, te whakahaeretanga hoki o te kōhanga reo i runga anō i ngā whakaritenga whakahaere e tika ana, ā, me whakawhanake, me pupuri, me whakapūmau, me arotake hoki i ia te wā ngā tuhinga me ngā mauhanga e tika ana, ā, me noho wātea i ngā wā e tika ana hei tirohanga mā tētahi matua o ngā tamariki i whakaurua ai ki te ratonga, ā, i ngā wā katoa ka tono tētahi e whakatutuki ana i ngā mahi i raro i te Wāhanga 26 o te ture[R47 (1-2) Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; GMA 1-12 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]
  • whakarite i te whakatutukitanga o te kōhanga reo i ngā tikanga katoa e pā ana ki ngā mahi arowhai a ngā pirihimana i ngā kaimahi, mō te noho haumaru me te ārai hoki o ngā tamariki[Wāhanga 319A Te Ture Mātauranga 1989]
  • whakarite i te whiwhinga o rātou e tiaki ana i ngā whara me ngā māuiui o ngā tamariki, i tētahi tohu whakaora whāwhai, mai i tētahi ratonga whakangungu i whakamanatia ai e Te Mana Tohu Mātauranga o Aotearoa[R46 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; HS 24-30 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]
  • whakarite i te whakaatu, te whakatinanatanga hoki o te tukanga whakamoe tamariki, ā, me tuhi, me pupuri hoki i ngā wā moe o ngā tamariki[R46 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; HS 9-11 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]
  • arotake i ia te wā i ngā āhuatanga mōrearea ki ngā taputapu, ngā whare me ngā rauhanga mō ngā tamariki, ā, me whakapau kaha ki te whakakore, te aukati, te whakaiti rānei i ngā āhuatanga e noho mōrearea ana ki ngā tamariki[R46 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; HS 12-18 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]
  • whakarite i te hāngaitanga o te kōhanga reo ki ngā taumata katoa o te hauora me te haumaru mō te noho haumaru o ngā tamariki ki te kōhanga reo, ā, me whakatinana i ngā tukanga e tika ana inā he ahi, he rū whenua, he āhuatanga ohorere rānei[R46 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; HS 1, 4-8 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]
  • whakarite i te noho haumaru o ngā tamariki nā te aukati i ngā wāhi o te whare kāhore e tutuki ana i ngā herenga ā-ture, kāhore rānei e tutuki ana i ngā ture ā-rohe o te kaunihera me te Ture Hanga Whare 2004, ā, me whakatika i ngā āhuatanga katoa kāhore e tutuki ana i ngā herenga ā-ture, kia noho haumaru ai ngā tamariki me rātou e taetae mai ana ki te kōhanga reo.[R45 Ture Mātauranga (Kōhungahunga) 2008; PF 3-4 Paearu Raihana mō ngā Kōhanga Reo kei raro i te maru o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo 2008]

4 Ngā Taunakitanga

I whakawhanakehia e Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga me te whānau o te kōhanga reo ngā taunakitanga e whai ake nei, arā, kia:

  • whakahaere tonu Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo i te kōhanga reo, tae noa ki te wā kua tau ake te whakahaeretanga a te whānau, kua whai hua hoki ngā whakahaeretanga mō ngā tamariki, ā, kua hāngai ngā whakaritenga ki ngā whāinga o Te Korowai
  • whiwhi ngā kaimahi i te pakari, te auau hoki o ngā ārahitanga me te aroturukitanga a Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo, hei whakapai ake i ngā mahi ngaio.

Te Whakarāpopototanga

E tū ana Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Maunga Kōhungahunga ki Papakura, ki Tāmaki-makau-rau ki te tonga. Kei te noho āwangawanga Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga mō te mātauranga me te atawhai o ngā tamariki. Nā te ngoikore o te whakahaeretanga me ngā hononga, kāhore e arotahi mārika ana ki ngā tamariki. I tēnei wā, kei te whakahaere ngā kaupapa kaimahi ā-rohe o Te Poari Matua o Ngā Kōhanga Reo i te kōhanga reo.

Hei ā hea Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga arotake anō ai i te kōhanga reo?

Ka arotake anō Te Tari Arotake Mātauranga i Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Maunga Kōhungahunga i roto i te kotahi tau.

1 Context

Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Maunga Kōhungahunga is in Papakura, South Auckland. The previous ERO report identified that whānau members had made some progress to improve management and staff performance.

In 2015, Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust (TKRNT) district staff recognised that whānau had not sustained improvement and needed further support to manage the kōhanga reo. TKRNT intervened and have begun to refocus the whānau on Te Korowai and their responsibilities for the care and learning of children. ERO’s focus for the review was on the quality of management to support children’s wellbeing.

2 The Review Findings

To what extent do whānau understand their roles and responsibilities for supporting children?

Whānau have a limited understanding of their roles and responsibilities.

Te Korowai

Poor whānau management of the kōhanga reo has hindered children’s learning. For the last two years staffing has been inconsistent and whānau turnover is high. Whānau induction has not been effective as new whānau are unsure of their roles and responsibilities. There is no strategic plan to provide future direction for the kōhanga reo. Decisions made by whānau are ill informed and do not focus on children. The kōhanga reo is in a vulnerable financial position. Poor communication and management practices have contributed to significant conflict and confusion. ERO is not confident that children will develop as confident, happy and emotionally secure learners in this environment.

The charter is not clear or useful. Most policies and procedures are outdated and do not provide sufficient guidance to support health and safety. Health and safety duties by kaimahi are not fully completed. Children’s safety cannot be assured while at kōhanga reo.

Administration systems are poor. No resource has been allocated to support the day to day administration and operational tasks. Kōhanga reo documents are difficult to locate. Parents are unable to contact the kōhanga reo during operating hours and basic tasks are neglected.

Personnel management is inadequate. There has been no appraisal of staff. Newly appointed kaimahi are not attested through TKRNT and police vetting procedures are incomplete. Programme planning practices across the kōhanga reo are not well developed.

Children’s physical safety is a concern. Extensions made to the existing kōhanga reo building do not comply with relevant regulations. There is no documentation for whānau permission to administer medicines. Earthquake, fire drills and hazard and property maintenance are not regularly carried out and recorded.

3 Whānau Management Assurance on Compliance Areas

During the review, ERO looked at the kōhanga reo systems for managing the following areas that have a potentially high impact on children's wellbeing:

  • emotional safety (including positive guidance and child protection)
  • physical safety (including supervision; sleep procedures; accidents; medication; hygiene; excursion policies and procedures)
  • suitable staffing (including qualification levels; police vetting; teacher registration; ratios)
  • evacuation procedures and practices for fire and earthquake.

All kōhanga reo are required to promote children's health and safety and to regularly review their compliance with legal requirements.

During the course of this ERO review many concerns with non-compliance were identified. The whānau with their licensee must:

  • ensure that suitable human resource management practices and processes are implemented, and those employed or engaged in the service are provided with adequate professional support, professional development opportunities[R47 (1-2) Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; GMA 7 Licensing Criteria for Te Kōhanga Reo affiliated to Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]
  • ensure that medicine (prescription and non-prescription) is not given to a child unless it is given by a doctor or ambulance personnel in an emergency; or by the parent of the child; or with the written authority (appropriate to the category of medicine) of a parent, and adults who administer medicine to children (other than their own) are provided with information and/or training relevant to the task[R46 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; HS 24-30 Licensing Criteria for Te Kōhanga Reo affiliated to Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]
  • ensure that the kōhanga reo is effectively governed and is managed in accordance with good management practices; and appropriate documentation and records are developed, kept, maintained, and regularly reviewed; and made available where appropriate at any reasonable time on request by a parent of a child enrolled in the service; and at any time on request by any person exercising powers or carrying out functions under Part 26 of the Act[R47 (1-2) Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; GMA 1-12 Licensing Criteria for Te Kōhanga Reo affiliated to Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]
  • ensure that the kōhanga reo complies with all requirements for police vetting for the safety and protection of children[Section 319A Education Act 1989]
  • ensure that those attending to children’s injuries, and / or illnesses holds a current first Aid qualification gained from a New Zealand Qualifications Authority accredited first aid training provider, or meets these qualification requirements[R46 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; HS 24-30 Licensing Criteria for Te Kōhanga Reo affiliated to Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]
  • ensure that a procedure for monitoring children’s sleep is displayed and implemented and a record of children’s sleep times is kept[R46 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; HS 9-11 Licensing Criteria for Te Kōhanga Reo affiliated to Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]
  • ensure that equipment, premises, and facilities are regularly checked for hazards to children and all practicable steps are taken to eliminate, isolate, or minimise hazards to the safety of children[R46 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; HS 12-18 Licensing Criteria for Te Kōhanga Reo affiliated to Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]
  • ensure that the kōhanga reo complies with all health and safety practices standards for children’s safety at the kōhanga reo, and that appropriate procedures are in place to deal with fires, earthquakes, and other emergencies[R46 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; HS 1, 4-8 Licensing Criteria for Te Kōhanga Reo affiliated to Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]
  • ensure that children are kept safe from building alterations that have not met compliance standards and have not met relevant bylaws of the local authority and the Building Act 2004, and address all outstanding non compliances for the safety of children and those attending the kōhanga reo.[R45 Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008; PF 3-4 Licensing Criteria for Te Kōhanga Reo affiliated to Te Kōhanga Reo National Trust 2008]

4 Recommendation

ERO and the Kōhanga Reo whānau with TKRNT developed the following recommendations:

TKRNT continue management of the kōhanga reo until such time whānau management is stabilised, effectively operating for children and practices reflect the goals of Te Korowai

employees receive intensive and regular mentoring and monitoring by TKRNT to improve professional practice.

Conclusion

Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Maunga Kōhungahunga is in Papakura, South Auckland. ERO is concerned for children’s education and care. Poor management and relationships has prevented the focus on children. TKRNT regional staff currently manage the kōhanga reo.

When is ERO likely to review the kōhanga reo again?

The next ERO review of Te Kōhanga Reo o Te Maunga Kōhungahunga will be within one year.
