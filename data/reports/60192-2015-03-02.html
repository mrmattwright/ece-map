1 Evaluation of Grafton Private Kindergarten

Very well placed

Grafton Private Kindergarten is well placed to promote positive learning outcomes for children.ERO's findings that support this overall judgement are summarised below.

Background

Grafton Private Kindergarten is located in the Kapiti community of Paraparaumu. The service has been operating for 25 years. Children and families come to this kindergarten from a wide geographical area, from Raumati South through to North Beach, Paraparaumu.

The education and care service is privately owned, operated and managed by the owner. A newly-established collaborative leadership model has been set up. Two centre supervisors lead the day-to-day operations of the kindergarten. The all-day service is licensed for up to¬†32 children aged over two years. Of the 55 children enrolled six are MƒÅori.¬†

Respectful relationships between staff, parents and families support children's learning.The identified centre strengths in the March 2012 ERO report have been sustained. Recommendations and areas identified for further development at that time have been progressed.

The Review Findings

Children experience positive, warm and caring relationships with teachers and other children. Wellbeing and social competence are prioritised and supported. The rhythms of the children are observed, and teacher interactions and interventions are responsive and respectful.

The tone is settled and calm. Teachers know the children well, highlighting their individual needs, interests and strengths. A well-considered approach to encouraging children‚Äôs independence is evident.

Children demonstrate confidence and capability in leading their own learning. The programme offers opportunities for individual preference and choice. Teacher-led and child-led learning are integral to centre practice. A wide range of open-ended resources successfully supports children‚Äôs learning through exploration and creativity.

Mathematics, numeracy and literacy skills are intentionally planned for and learning experiences for children are enriched through daily routines and experiences.

Management Assurance on Legal Requirements

Before the review, the staff and management of Grafton Private Kindergarten completed an ERO Centre Assurance Statement and Self-Audit Checklist. In these documents they attested that they have taken all reasonable steps to meet their legal obligations related to:

  • curriculum
  • premises and facilities
  • health and safety practices
  • governance, management and administration.

During the review, ERO looked at the service‚Äôs systems for managing the following areas that have a potentially high impact on children's wellbeing:

  • evacuation procedures and practices for fire and earthquake.

All early childhood services are required to promote children's health and safety and to regularly review their compliance with legal requirements.
