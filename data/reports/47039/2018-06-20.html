Background

New Shoots Children‚Äôs Centre (Sandhurst) is located in Papamoa. It is licensed to provide all-day education and care for 75 children including 28 aged under two. The current roll of 92 includes 15 children who identify as MƒÅori and 23 children from a range of other cultural heritages.

There are four aged-based rooms within the centre to cater for children aged 3 months to school age. The centre philosophy emphasises the development of relationships based on integrity, respect, nurture and trust. This centre is privately owned by three educators who are qualified in early childhood education and also own and govern centres in Tauranga, Auckland and Hamilton. This is the first ERO evaluation for this centre.

  • premises and facilities
  • health and safety practices
  • governance, management and administration.

All early childhood services are required to promote children's health and safety and to regularly review their compliance with legal requirements.
